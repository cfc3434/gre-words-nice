mediocre [ˌmiːdiˈoʊkər] 【考法】 adj．平庸的；质量中等偏下的：moderate to inferior in quality; ordinary 例 Without a desire for success, life is at best mediocre．没有对成功的强烈渴望，人生最多也就是碌碌无为。 近 average, common, commonplace, fair, indifferent, medium, ordinary 反 outstanding, excellent, exceptional, extraordinary, impressive, superior　优秀的 派 mediocrity n．平庸者；平庸
