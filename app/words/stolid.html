stolid [ˈstɑːlɪd] 【考法】 adj．无动于衷的，感情麻木的：having or revealing little emotion or sensibility 例 Her face showed nothing but stolid indifference．她的脸上只露出了麻木和冷漠。 近 apathetic, catatonic, deadpan, emotionless, impassive, indifferent, numb, phlegmatic, stoic 反 demonstrative, emotional, fervent, fervid, impassioned, passionate, vehement　充满热情的 派 stolidity n．麻木，无动于衷
