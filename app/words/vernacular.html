vernacular [vərˈnækjələr] 【考法1】 n．方言：a nonstandard language or dialect of a place, region, or country 例 phrases that occur in the common vernacular　常见方言中的词组 近 argot, cant, dialect, jargon, lingo, slang 【考法2】 adj．非正式的，口头的：used in or suitable for speech and not formal writing 例 write essays in a very easy-to-read, vernacular style　用一种易懂的口头化风格写作 近 conversational, informal, nonliterary, vulgar 反 bookish, formal, learned, literary　书面的，正式的
