requite [rɪˈkwaɪt] 【考法1】 vt．酬谢，报答：to make repayment or return for 例 requite her love with hatred　恩将仇报 近 indemnify, reciprocate, recompense, recoup, reimburse, remunerate, repay 【考法2】 vt．报仇：to punish in kind the wrongdoer responsible for 例 The future writer would later requite the abuse he suffered at the hands of his classmates by creating scathing portraits of them in his novels．未来的作家会把在同学那里受了委屈的账算清的——他会在小说中把他们描绘成恶人。 近 avenge, redress, retaliate, revenge 反 absolve, condone, forgive, pardon　饶恕，宽恕
