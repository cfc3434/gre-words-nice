nomad [ˈnoʊmæd] 【考法】 adj./n．游牧的；居无定所的人：a member of a people who have no fixed residence but move from place to place usually seasonally and within a well-defined territory 例 a homeless nomad　一个无家可归、居无定所的人 近 ambulant, fugitive, gallivanting, perambulatory, peripatetic, ranging, roaming, vagabond, vagrant, wandering, wayfaring 反 settled　定居的
