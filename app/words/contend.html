contend [kənˈtend] 【考法1】 vi．竞争，争夺：to strive or vie in contest or rivalry or against difficulties 例 two traditional rivals contending for the championship　两个争夺冠军头衔的老对手 近 battle, fight, race, rival, vie 反 capitulate, quit, succumb, surrender, give in　投降 【考法2】 vt．声明，声称：to state as a fact usually forcefully 例 I would contend that the minister's thinking is flawed on this point．我声明部长的想法在这一点上有漏洞。 近 affirm, allege, assert, aver, avouch, avow, declare, insist, maintain, profess, protest, purport, warrant 反 deny, gainsay　否认
