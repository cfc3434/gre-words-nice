deterioration [dɪˌtɪriəˈreɪʃn] 【考法】 n．恶化；堕落：a gradual sinking and wasting away of mind or body 例 a continuing deterioration in relations between the two countries　两国关系的持续恶化 近 decadence, degeneration, deterioration, devolution, downfall, downgrade 反 improvement, recovery, recuperation, rehabilitation, revitalization　改进，改善
