provisory [prəˈvaɪzəri] 【考法】 adj．有附带条件的；临时的：depending on a proviso; conditional; serving in a position for the time being 例 a provisory permit to block off the street while movie scenes were being shot　一个同意在电影拍摄期间封锁道路的临时许可 近 interim, provisional, provisionary, provisory 反 unconditional, long-term, permanent　无条件的，永久的
