appeal [əˈpiːl] 【考法1】 n．申请：an application (as to a recognized authority) for corroboration, vindication, or decision 例 a piteous appeal for help　哀怜地请求帮助 ‖ make an appeal to the public to donate needed blood　向公众提出献血请求 近 adjuration, conjuration, entreaty, petition, pleading, supplication 【考法2】 n．起诉：to charge with a crime: accuse 例 My lawyer said the court's decision wasn't correct and that we should file for an appeal．我的律师说，法庭的判决不对，我们应该起诉。 近 charge, accuse, incriminate, inculpate, indict 反 absolve　赦免；exonerate, exculpate　开脱罪责；vindicate　辩护
