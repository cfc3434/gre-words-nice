dispassionate [dɪsˈpæʃənət] 【考法】 adj．客观公正的，不易被情绪或偏见影响的：devoid of or unaffected by passion, emotion, or bias 例 a dispassionate analysis　客观公正的分析 近 disinterested, equal, equitable, evenhanded, impartial, just, nonpartisan, objective, unbiased, unprejudiced 反 biased, inequitable, nonobjective, one-sided, partial, partisan, prejudiced, unjust　有偏见的，不公正的
