kindred [ˈkɪndrəd] 【考法】 adj．类似的；具有相似或相近的起源、本性或性质的：having a similar or related origin, nature, or character 例 She finally found people who were kindred spirits when she joined the hiking club．她加入登山俱乐部的时候终于发现了志向相同的人。 近 agreeable, amicable, compatible, congenial, frictionless, unanimous, united 反 disagreeable, discordant, disharmonious, disunited, incompatible, inharmonious, uncongenial　不一致的，不和谐的
