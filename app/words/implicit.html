implicit [ɪmˈplɪsɪt] 【考法】 adj．不言而喻的，心照不宣的：capable of being understood from something else though unexpressed 例 The implicit agreement among members of the outing club is that everyone pays his or her own way on all trips．野外俱乐部成员之间一条心照不宣的约定就是：沿途所有开销都AA。 近 implied, unexpressed, unspoken, unvoiced, wordless 反 explicit, expressed, spoken, stated, voiced　明确声明的
