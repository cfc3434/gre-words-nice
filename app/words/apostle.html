apostle [əˈpɑːsl] 【考法】 n．（政策或思想等的）信奉者，支持者：a person who actively supports or favors a cause 例 Her mother was adedicated apostle of healthy eating．她母亲是健康饮食矢志不渝的倡导者。 近 advocate, champion, crusader, expounder, espouser, exponent, partisan, promoter, proponent, protagonist, supporter, zealot 反 adversary, antagonist, opponent　反对者
