laconic [ləˈkɑːnɪk] 【考法】 adj．简洁（以至于显得粗鲁或难以理解）的：using or involving the use of a minimum of words: concise to the point of seeming rude or mysterious 例 His mentor's comment tends to be laconic but very much to the point．他导师的点评很简短，但是却总能说到点子上。 近 apothegmatic, brief, capsule, compact, compendious, curt, pithy, succinct, summary, telegraphic, terse 反 circuitous, circumlocutory, diffuse, prolix, rambling, verbose, windy, wordy　冗长的
