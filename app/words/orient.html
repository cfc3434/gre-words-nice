orient [ˈɔːriənt] 【考法】 vt．使确定方向，使熟悉或适应：to set or arrange in any determinate position especially in relation to the points of the compass; to make familiar with or adjusted to facts, principles, or a situation 例 orient students toward a career in medicine　指导学生从事医学的职业道路 近 accustom, familiarize, initiate, introduce, orientate 反 confuse　使迷惑
