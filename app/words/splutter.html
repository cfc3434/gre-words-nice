splutter [ˈsplʌtər] 【考法】 vi．急切而不清楚地说：to speak hastily and incoherently, as when confused or angry 例 They begin to splutter and move restlessly about if they feel time is slipping away without some return．当他们感到时间在悄悄流逝却一无所得时，他们便开始激动地说话，不安地走动。 近 babble, drivel, gabble, gibber, jabber, sputter, stammer, stutter 反 articulate, enunciate, pronounce　清楚地说
