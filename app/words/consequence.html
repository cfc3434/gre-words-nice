consequence [ˈkɑːnsəkwens] 【考法1】 n．结果：something produced by a cause or necessarily following from a set of conditions 例 negative consequences of the war　战争带来的负面后果 近 aftermath, effect, outcome, result 反 source, origin, cause, antecedent　根源，原因 【考法2】 n．重要性，价值：significance; importance 例 a mistake of no consequence　不重要的错误 近 moment, magnitude, weight 反 triviality　琐碎的事 派 consequential adj．重要的
