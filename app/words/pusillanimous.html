pusillanimous [ˌpjuːsɪˈlænɪməs] 【考法】 adj．懦弱的，胆小得令人鄙视的：lacking courage and resolution, marked by contemptible timidity 例 pusillanimous politicians who vote according to whichever way the political wind is blowing　胆小懦弱的政客，舆论风吹向谁就投票给谁 近 craven, dastardly, gutless, poltroon, recreant, spineless 反 brave, courageous, daring, dauntless, doughty, gallant, gutsy, intrepid, lionhearted, stalwart, stout, stouthearted, valiant, valorous　勇敢的
