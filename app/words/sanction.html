sanction [ˈsæŋkʃn] 【考法】 vt．批准，同意，认可：to make valid or binding usually by a formal procedure (as ratification) 例 The President sanctioned covert operations．总统批准了秘密行动。‖ The administration will sanction almost any field trip with educational value．基本上任何有教育意义的实地考察都可以得到批准。 近 accredit, approbate, authorize, certify, confirm, finalize, formalize, license, ratify, warrant 反 interdict, prohibit, proscribe　禁止；decline, deny, disallow, disapprove, negative, reject, veto　否决
