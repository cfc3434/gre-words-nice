committed [kəˈmɪtɪd] 【考法】 adj．忠诚的，忠实的：loyal to a belief, organization, or group, and willing to work hard for it 例 remain committed to one's youthful ideal　坚持不懈地追求自己年轻时的理想 近 loyal, faithful, allegiant 反 disloyal　不忠诚的 派 noncommittal adj．不明确的：giving no clear indication of attitude or feeling 例 a noncommittal reply　一个不明确的回答 反 confirmable　确定的
