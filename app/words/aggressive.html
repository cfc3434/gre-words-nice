aggressive [əˈɡresɪv] 【考法1】 adj．好斗的：having a quality of anger and determination that makes it ready to attack others 例 aggressive behavior　具有攻击性的行为 近 fierce, assaultive, combative, militant, confrontational, go-getting, self-assertive, truculent, pugnacious 反 even-tempered, nonbelligerent, pacific, uncombative, uncontentious　心平气和的，不好斗的 【考法2】 adj．强有力的，强烈的：marked by or uttered with forcefulness 例 an aggressive campaign to win the African-American vote　一次能够获得非裔美国人选票的强有力的竞选 近 dynamic, energetic, full-blooded, vigorous
