vindicate [ˈvɪndɪkeɪt] 【考法1】 vt．为…平反，为…辩护，使无罪：to free from allegation or blame 例 The evidence would completely vindicate him.　这个证据将彻底证明他的清白。 近 absolve, acquit, exculpate, exonerate 反 incriminate　使有罪 【考法2】 vt．证明，证实：to give evidence or testimony to the truth or factualness of 例 Recent discoveries have generally vindicated the physicist's theories．近期的发现整体上证明了物理学家的理论。 近 attest, authenticate, certify, corroborate, substantiate, support, validate, verify 反 disprove, rebut, refute　反驳，驳斥
