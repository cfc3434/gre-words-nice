innocuous [ɪˈnɑːkjuəs] 【考法1】 adj．无害的：producing no injury 例 The government enacted a more strict regulation on innocuous preservatives．政府对无害防腐剂制定了更为严格的规定。 近 anodyne, benign, harmless, inoffensive, nontoxic, safe 反 damaging, detrimental, harmful, injurious, noxious, pernicious, invasive　有害的 【考法2】 adj．不会引起敌意的：not likely to give offense or to arouse strong feelings or hostility 例 He made an innocuous remark to avoid conflict．为了避免冲突，他做了一个中庸的评价。 近 bland, inoffensive, insipid, neutral, sapless 反 provoking　刺激性的
