rapprochement [ˌræproʊʃˈmɑːn] 【考法】 n．和睦，友好：establishment of or state of having cordial relations 例 a new era of rapprochement between China and Russia　中俄两国睦邻友好关系的新纪元 近 amity, communion, concord, fellowship, harmony, rapport 反 animosity, antagonism, antipathy, bitterness, enmity, hostility, jaundice, rancor　敌意
