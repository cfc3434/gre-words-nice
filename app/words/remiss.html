remiss [rɪˈmɪs] 【考法】 adj．疏忽的，不留心的：exhibiting carelessness or slackness 例 I would be remiss if I did not do something about it．如果我对此不做点什么就是懈怠。 近 careless, derelict, disregardful, heedless, lax, lazy, neglectful, neglecting, slack 反 attentive, careful, conscientious, meticulous, painstaking, scrupulous　细致的，仔细的
