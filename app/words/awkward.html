awkward [ˈɔːkwərd] 【考法1】 adj．缺乏灵活性和技巧的：lacking dexterity or skill 例 awkward dance step　笨拙的舞步 近 handless, maladroit 反 adroit, deft, dexterous, proficient, skilled　灵巧的 【考法2】 adj．（处理问题）缺乏手段和智谋的：showing or marked by a lack of skill and tact (as in dealing with a situation) 例 Her awkward handling of the seating arrangements resulted in many hurt feelings．她那缺乏智谋的座次安排伤害了许多人的感情。 近 botched, bungling, fumbled, inept, inexpert, maladroit
