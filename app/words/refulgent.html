refulgent [rɪˈfʌldʒənt] 【考法】 adj．辉煌的，灿烂的：shining radiantly; resplendent 例 Refulgent sunlight broke through the clouds, creating huge swaths of light in the valley below us．耀眼的阳光穿透了云层，在我们脚下的峡谷中投下了一块巨大的光斑。 近 beaming, brilliant, dazzling, incandescent, lucid, luminous, lustrous, radiant, resplendent, splendid 反 dim, dull, lackluster　黯淡无光的 派 refulgence n．灿烂，光芒四射
