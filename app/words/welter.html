welter [ˈweltər] 【考法】 n．混乱，动乱：a state of wild disorder 例 There was a welter of pushing and shoving．到处是推搡和拥挤的混乱局面。‖ The troop withdrawal would plunge the country into a welter of anarchy and endless civil war．撤军会使得这个国家陷入无政府的动乱状态和无尽的内战中。 近 disturbance, furor, hurricane, pandemonium, turmoil, uproar, whirl 反 calm, peace, tranquility　平静，宁静；order　秩序
