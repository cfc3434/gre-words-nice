shroud [ʃraʊd] 【考法】 vt．将…从视线中隔离，遮蔽：to shut off from sight; screen 例 She shrouded the fact that the child had been adopted．她将孩子是领养的这个事实隐藏起来。 近 belie, blanket, cloak, conceal, cover, curtain, disguise, mask, occult, screen, veil, blot out, paper over 反 bare, disclose, display, divulge, expose, reveal, show　展示，暴露
