accommodate [əˈkɑːmədeɪt] 【考法1】 v．改变以适应新情况、新场景：to change (something) so as to make it suitable for a new use or situation 例 accommodate to the life overseas　适应海外的生活 近 acclimate, adjust, condition, conform, doctor, edit, shape, suit, tailor 【考法2】 vt．使和谐：to bring to a state free of conflicts, inconsistencies, or differences 例 The idea that the United States could harmoniously accommodate all was a fiction．认为美国能够和谐一切的想法只是幻想。 近 attune, conciliate, conform, coordinate, reconcile 反 disharmonize　使不和谐
