spur [spзːr] 【考法】 n．刺激物：something that arouses action or activity vt．刺激，激励：to incite or stimulate 例 Appreciation spurred his ambition．赞扬激起了他的雄心壮志。 近 boost, catalyst, impetus, incentive, incitement, instigation, momentum, motivation, provocation, stimulus; arouse, excite, exhort, goad, instigate, prod, stir, urge 反 deterrent, disincentive　阻碍物；bridle, check, constrain, curb, deter, fetter, hamper, inhibit, manacle, shackle　制止，阻碍，束缚
