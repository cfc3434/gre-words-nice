surpass [sərˈpæs] 【考法】 vt．超越，强于：to become better, greater, or stronger than 例 She always tried to surpass her older brother at anything he did, which results in his diffidence．她在任何一个方面都要比她哥哥优秀，由此导致了他自信心的缺乏。 近 beat, better, eclipse, exceed, excel, outclass, outmatch, outshine, outstrip, overtop, transcend 反 fall behind　落后
