transgress [trænzˈɡres] 【考法】 vt．违背；犯错：to fail to keep; to commit an offense 例 Don't even think about transgressing the drug laws of that Asian country, for punishments are severe and there's nothing that our government can do to intervene．别指望违背那个亚洲国家的药品相关法，因为惩罚非常严厉，我们政府完全无法干涉。 近 breach, break, contravene, fracture, infringe, offend, traduce 反 follow, mind, obey, observe, comply with, conform to　遵守
