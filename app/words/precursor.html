precursor [priːˈkзːrsər] 【考法】 n．先驱者，先导：one that precedes and indicates the approach of another 例 18th-century lyric poets like Robert Burns were precursors of the Romantics．十八世纪的抒情诗人（如罗伯特·彭斯）是浪漫主义的先驱。 近 foregoer, forerunner, harbinger, herald, outrider 反 sequela, successor　后继者；descendant　后代 派 precursory adj．先驱性的，开创性的
