consilience [kənˈsɪliəns] 【考法】 n．融合：the linking together of principles from different disciplines especially when forming a comprehensive theory 例 The philosopher of science Whewell called this process of independent lines of inquiry converging together to a conclusion a“consilience of inductions”．科学哲学家修艾尔，就把这种经由不同研究途径得到万流归一的结论过程，称作“归纳的融通”。
