constitute [ˈkɑːnstətuːt] 【考法1】 vt．指派，任命：to appoint to an office, function, or dignity 例 He was constituted manager．他被任命为经理。 近 nominate, designate, authorize 反 discharge, dismiss　撤除职务；abdicate, resign　退位，辞职 【考法2】 vt．构成：make up, form, compose 例 Water constitutes the greater part of the human body．水分构成人体的很大部分。 近 compose, comprise, form 派 constitution n．构成；宪法；体格
