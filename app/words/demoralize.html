demoralize [dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪz] 【考法1】 vt．使士气低落：to undermine the confidence or morale of; dishearten 例 demoralize the staff　使员工士气低落 近 daunt, dishearten, dismay, dispirit, unnerve 反 invigorate, cheer　鼓舞 【考法2】 vt．贬低，使堕落：to lower in character, dignity, or quality 例 We refused to be demoralized by our humiliating defeat．我们拒绝由于这场屈辱的战败而被贬低。 近 abase, corrupt, debauch, degrade, demean 反 elevate, ennoble, uplift　使崇高，提升
