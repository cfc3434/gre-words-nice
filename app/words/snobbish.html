snobbish [ˈsnɑːbɪʃ] 【考法】 adj．谄上傲下的，自大的：being or characteristic of a person who has an offensive air of superiority and tends to ignore or disdain anyone regarded as inferior 例 snobbish belief/smile/attitude　自大的想法/微笑/态度 近 aristocratic, bumptious, elitist, haughty, imperious, persnickety, pompous, presumptuous, supercilious 反 egoless, humble, modest　谦逊的，不自大的
