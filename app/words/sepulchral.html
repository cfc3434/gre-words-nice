sepulchral [səˈpʌlkrəl] 【考法】 adj．阴沉的；丧葬的：causing or marked by an atmosphere lacking in cheer; funereal 例 The decrepit mansion had a sepulchral tone that gave everybody a chill．破旧的公馆有着一种坟墓的气息，让人皆为之一颤。 近 depressing, desolate, dismal, dreary, morose, sullen, tenebrous 反 merry, bright, cheery, comforting, cordial, festive, heartwarming, sunshiny　欢快的
