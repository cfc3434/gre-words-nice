disregard [ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrd] 【考法1】 vt．漠视，不关注：to pay no attention to 例 disregard his feelings　漠视他的感受 近 ignore, overlook, slight 反 heed, mind, regard, attend to　关注 【考法2】 n．缺乏兴趣，缺乏关心：lack of interest or concern 例 Revelers fired guns in the air with complete disregard for the possible consequences．饮酒狂欢者朝空中乱开枪，一点也不关心可能产生的后果。 近 apathy, disinterestedness, incuriosity, nonchalance, torpor, unconcern 反 concern, interest, regard　关心，感兴趣
