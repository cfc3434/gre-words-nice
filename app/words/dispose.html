dispose [dɪˈspoʊz] 【考法1】 vt．使倾向于：to give a tendency to: incline 例 faulty diet disposes one to sickness　营养不全面导致疾病 派 disposed adj．喜欢的：willing or likely, inclined 例 not feel disposed to argue with her　不喜欢与她争执 近 willing, inclined, predisposed, prone 反 disinclined, indisposed　不喜欢的 【考法2】 vi．处理掉（与of连用，dispose of）：to get rid of; throw out 派 disposal n．处理，不再利用：the act of getting rid of something that is no longer wanted or needed 例 the permanent disposal of radioactive wastes　对放射性废物的永久处理
