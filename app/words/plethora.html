plethora [ˈpleθərə] 【考法】 n．过量，过剩：excess, superfluity 例 a plethora of advice and a paucity of assistance　给出大量建议而鲜有实质性援助 近 abundance, cornucopia, feast, overabundance, overflow, plentitude, profusion, surfeit, surplus 反 dearth, inadequacy, insufficiency, paucity, scarcity, undersupply　缺乏 派 plethoric adj．过量的
