repulse [rɪˈpʌls] 【考法】 vt．使厌恶，排斥：to rebuff or reject with rudeness, coldness, or denial 例 The scenes of violence in the film may repulse some viewers．电影中某些暴力场景可能会使观众反感。 近 disgust, nauseate, repel, revolt, sicken 反 allure, attract, bewitch, captivate, charm, enchant, entice, fascinate, lure, seduce, tempt　吸引，诱惑 派 repulsion n．排斥
