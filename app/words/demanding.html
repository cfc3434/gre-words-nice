demanding [dɪˈmændɪŋ] 【考法1】 adj．难取悦的，难满足的：not easily satisfied or pleased 例 His mother could be demanding at times．他母亲有时候很难取悦。 近 exacting, fastidious, finical, finicking, fussy, picky 【考法2】 adj．费时间花心思的：requiring much time, effort, or careful attention 例 The demanding assignment kept them working all night long．那项劳神的任务使得他们熬夜。 近 arduous, burdensome, challenging, exacting, grueling, killing, laborious, onerous, persnickety, taxing, toilsome 反 light, unchallenging, undemanding　不费劲的，轻而易举的
