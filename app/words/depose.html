depose [dɪˈpoʊz] 【考法1】 vi．宣誓作证：to testify to under oath or by affidavit 例 He was nervous when the time to depose before the jury finally arrived．他很紧张，在陪审团面前作证的时刻终于到来了。 近 attest, swear 反 perjure　作伪证 【考法2】 vt．废黜，罢免：to remove from a throne or other high position 例 amilitary junta deposed the dictator after he had bankrupted the country　军阀罢黜了使国家陷入赤贫的独裁者 近 defrock, deprive, oust, uncrown 反 crown, enthrone　授予王权
