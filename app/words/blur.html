blur [blзːr] 【考法1】 v．（使）变得朦胧，（使）变得不清楚：to（cause sth. to）become vague or indistinct 例 Sorrowful tears blurred her eyes．悲伤的眼泪模糊了她的双眼。 近 shroud, becloud, befog, obscure 反 clear　使清晰 派 blurring adj．朦胧的 【考法2】 vt．使不易理解：to make (something) unclear to the understanding 例 An article for the layman that blurs the distinction between the two kinds of cholesterol．一篇针对普通读者的文章对两种胆固醇的区别表述得含糊不清。 近 obfuscate 反 clarify, illuminate　阐明，说清楚
