bumptious [ˈbʌmpʃəs] 【考法】 adj．专横傲慢的，自以为是的：having a feeling of superiority that shows itself in an overbearing attitude 例 be bumptious over one's inferiors　对下级态度傲慢 近 assumptive, supercilious, imperious, overweening, peremptory, pompous, presuming, presumptuous, self-assertive, haughty 反 humble, modest, unarrogant, unpretentious　谦虚的，低调的
