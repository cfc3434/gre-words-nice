efface [ɪˈfeɪs] 【考法】 vt．擦掉，抹去；使不明显：to eliminate or make indistinct by or as if by wearing away a surface 例 efface those unpleasant memories　抹去那些不快的记忆 近 eradicate, erase, expunge, exterminate, extirpate, liquidate, eclipse, obliterate, wipe, root out 反 blazon, decorate　装饰；emboss　使凸起，使显现 派 self-effacing adj．谦卑的，低调的
