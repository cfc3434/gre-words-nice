enunciate [ɪˈnʌnsieɪt] 【考法1】 v．清晰地说：to utter articulate sounds 例 She enunciates very slowly and carefully．她缓慢仔细清晰地说。 近 articulate 【考法2】 vt．公开宣布，宣称：to make known openly or publicly 例 Today the President enunciated a new foreign policy．总统在今天宣布了一项新的对外政策。 近 annunciate, broadcast, declare, herald, proclaim, promulgate, publicize, release, give out
