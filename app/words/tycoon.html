tycoon [taɪˈkuːn] 【考法】 n．大亨，巨头：a person of rank, power, or influence in a particular field 例 an oil tycoon who's widely considered the most powerful man in the county　被认为是郡上最有势力的一个石油大亨 ‖ The automobile tycoon is on the verge of bankruptcy．汽车巨头濒临破产的边缘。 近 baron, captain, king, lion, lord, magnate, mogul, monarch, prince 反 nobody, nothing, zero　不起眼的人物
