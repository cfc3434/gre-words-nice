vituperate [vaɪˈtuːpəreɪt] 【考法】 vt．谩骂，责骂：to abuse or censure severely or abusively 例 He was vituperated for betraying his friends．他因为背叛朋友而被责骂。 近 abuse, assail, belabor, berate, castigate, excoriate, lambaste, reprimand, scold, rail, revile, upbraid 反 admire, commend, laud, praise　赞赏 派 vituperative adj．辱骂的
