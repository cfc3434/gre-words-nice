deter [dɪˈtзːr] 【考法】 vt．吓住，威慑：to prevent or discourage from acting, as by means of fear or doubt 例 deter criminals from carrying guns　威慑罪犯使其不带枪 近 dissuade, inhibit 反 spur, encourage, persuade　激励 派 deterrent adj./n．威慑的；威慑力量 例 deterrent weapons　威慑性武器 ‖ a deterrent to theft　对偷窃的威慑 反 inducement, incentive　引诱，刺激
