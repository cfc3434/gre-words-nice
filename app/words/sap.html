sap [sæp] 【考法1】 vt．削弱：to weaken or exhaust the energy or vitality of 例 Weeks of hard work had sapped him and left him exhausted．连续几周辛苦工作让他削弱筋疲力尽。 近 debilitate, devitalize, enervate, enfeeble, etiolate, prostrate 反 bolster, fortify, invigorate, beef up　增强，使有活力 【考法2】 n．健康，活力：active strength of body or mind 例 a child full of sap and vivacity　活力满满的孩子 近 dynamism, energy, esprit, get-up-and-go, gusto, verve, vim, vitality 反 lethargy, listlessness, sluggishness, torpidity　无精打采
