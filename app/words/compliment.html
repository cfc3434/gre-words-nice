compliment n．[ˈkɑːmplɪmənt] v．[ˈkɑːmplɪment] 【考法1】 n./vt．称赞，恭维：an expression of praise, admiration, or congratulation 例 a man meriting the compliments and homage of his fellows　一个值得他的伙伴们尊敬和称赞的人 近 praise, commend, eulogize, extol, laud 反 vituperate　责骂 派 complimentary adj．称赞的：expressing or containing a compliment 反 vituperative　责骂的 【考法2】 n．敬意，免费赠送的礼物：formal and respectful recognition: honor 例 How about a delicious dessert then, with our compliment？给您上点甜点怎么样，算是我们小小的敬意。
