adjudicate [əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪt] 【考法】 v．裁决，判定：to hear and settle (a case, dispute or conflict) 例 When my wife and I asked the salesclerk to adjudicate our disagreement, she agreed with me that the white shoes looked better．我和我妻子要求售货员来裁决我们之间的争论，她同意我的观点，认为白色的鞋子看起来更漂亮。 近 adjudge, arbitrate, decide, determine, judge, referee, settle, umpire
