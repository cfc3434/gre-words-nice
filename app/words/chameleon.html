chameleon [kəˈmiːliən] 【考法】 n．变色龙，善变的人：a person who dexterously and expediently changes or adopts opinions 例 At the summer resort he acquired a reputation as a social chameleon—someone who could be whatever his hosts wanted him to be．在度假胜地，他被称为“社交多面手”——可以变成主人想要的任何角色。 近 chancer, opportunist, temporizer, timeserver, trimmer, weathercock
