cogent [ˈkoʊdʒənt] 【考法1】 adj．令人信服的：appealing forcibly to the mind or reason: convincing 例 Six Sigma is one of the most cogent methods for modern enterprises to control quality and optimizing process.“六西格玛”方法是现代企业进行质量控制和工艺优化最令人信服的方法之一。 近 convincing, compelling, conclusive, telling, persuasive, satisfying 反 unconvincing, unpersuasive　不令人信服的 【考法2】 adj．相关的：pertinent, relevant 例 a cogent analysis　一项相关的研究 近 apropos, germane, relative, relevant 反 extraneous, irrelevant, impertinent, irrelative　无关的
