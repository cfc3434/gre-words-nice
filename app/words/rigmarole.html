rigmarole [ˈrɪɡməroʊl] 【考法】 n．混乱而无意义的话：language marked by abstractions, jargon, euphemisms, and circumlocutions; confused or meaningless talk 例 The security guard gave me some kind of rigmarole about passes and authorizations．保安不知所云地跟我说了一些关于通关和授权的话。 近 abracadabra, babble, drivel, gabble, gibber, jabber, nonsense, prattle
