arrest [əˈrest] 【考法1】 n./v．停止：the stopping of a process or activity; to bring to a standstill 例 Science cannot yet arrest the process of aging．科学不能阻止衰老。 近 cease, cessation, closure, conclusion, discontinuance, discontinuation, ending, halt, stop, termination; check, stop, cease, close, conclude, discontinue, end, halt, terminate 反 continuance, continuation　继续 【考法2】 v．逮捕：to take or keep under one's control by authority of law 例 She was charged with resisting arrest．她因为拒捕受到起诉。 近 apprehend 反 discharge　释放 【考法3】 v．吸引（某人）注意力：to hold the attention of as if by a spell 例 The behavior of the daredevil arrested pedestrians．冒失鬼的行为吸引了行人们的注意力。 近 enchant, fascinate, bedazzle, grip, hypnotize, mesmerize
