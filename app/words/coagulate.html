coagulate [koʊˈæɡjuleɪt] 【考法】 v．（使）凝结，（使）变稠：to (cause to) become viscous or thickened into a coherent mass: curdle, clot 例 The blood coagulates to stop wounds bleeding．血液会凝结以防止伤口流血。 近 clot, congeal, jelly 反 melt, liquefy, fluidify, dissolve　融化，溶解；thin　使变稀薄 派 coagulant n．凝结剂：an agent that causes a liquid to coagulate
