blunt [blʌnt] 【考法1】 vt．使变钝：to make less sharp or definite 近 dull, deaden, hebetate, benumb, enfeeble 反 whet, sharpen　磨尖 【考法2】 adj．直率的：being or characterized by direct, brief, and potentially rude speech or manner 例 He values honesty and is quite blunt about telling people what he doesn't like about them．他重视诚实，经常直率地告诉别人他们何处使他不满。 近 abrupt, bluff, brusque, curt, gruff 反 circuitous, mealy-mouthed　拐弯抹角的
