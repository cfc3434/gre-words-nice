mirth [mзːrθ] 【考法】 n．欢乐，欢笑：gladness or gaiety as shown by or accompanied with laughter 例 a man of little mirth　不苟言笑之人 近 cheer, festivity, gaiety, glee, happiness, hilarity, jocundity, joviality, lightheartedness 反 dejection, depression, desolation, despondence, doldrums, melancholy, oppression　沮丧，忧伤 派 mirthful adj．高兴的
