hymn [hɪm] 【考法】 n．赞歌，赞美诗：a song of praise or joy v．赞美：to proclaim the glory of 例 They sang a hymn of praise to God．他们唱赞歌赞美上帝。‖ During the honeymoon following the inauguration, newspaper articles seemed to hymn the president's every move．在上任后的“蜜月期”之中，报纸新闻似乎在赞美总统的每一项措施。 近 eulogy, homage, hymn, ode, paean, panegyric, psalm, salutation, tribute; bless, carol, celebrate, emblazon, exalt, extol, glorify, laud, magnify, resound 反 dirge, elegy　哀诗，挽歌；blame, censure, reprehend, reprobate　责难，斥责
