pellucid [pəˈluːsɪd] 【考法】 adj．清晰明确的，易懂的：transparently clear in style or meaning 例 Her poetry has a pellucid simplicity that betrays none of the sweat that went into writing it．她的诗歌风格十分清新简约，丝毫没有体现出她为此付出的汗水。 近 apparent, distinct, evident, lucid, manifest, obvious, palpable, patent, perspicuous, plain, straightforward 反 clouded, cryptic, enigmatic, indistinct, mysterious, obfuscated, obscure　模糊难懂的
