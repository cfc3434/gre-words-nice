histrionic [ˌhɪstriˈɑːnɪk] 【考法】 adj．夸张做作的：dramatic, exaggerated, and insincere 例 Obama and McCain avoided a histrionic debate about China during the 2008 campaign and Obama probably thought he had a free hand on China policy．奥巴马和麦凯恩在2008年的选战中没有就中国进行做秀似的辩论，也许奥巴马就此认为他在对华政策上可以随心所欲。 近 dramatic, melodramatic, operatic, stagy, theatrical, affected 反 undramatic　没有戏剧化的
