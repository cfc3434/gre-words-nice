melodramatic [ˌmelədrəˈmætɪk] 【考法】 adj．感情夸张的，伤感的：exaggeratedly emotional or sentimental; histrionic 例 Our office drama queen yet again made the melodramatic declaration that she was contemplating suicide．我们办公室的“影后”又一次在危言耸听了：这回她说她准备自杀。 近 dramatic, hammy, histrionic, mannered, pretentious, stagy 反 nondramatic, nontheatrical　平淡无奇的，非戏剧性的
