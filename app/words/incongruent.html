incongruent [ɪnˈkɑːŋɡruənt] 【考法】 adj．不全等的；不一致的：not coinciding exactly when superimposed; not conforming to the circumstances or requirements of a situation 例 two incongruent triangles　两个不全等的三角形 近 conflicting, discordant, discrepant, dissonant, incompatible, incongruous, inconsonant 反 congruent, congruous, consistent　一致的
