counterfeit [ˈkaʊntərfɪt] 【考法1】 adj．仿制的，假冒的：made in imitation of something else with intent to deceive 例 counterfeit money　假币 近 forged, bogus, spurious, pseudo, feigned, artificial, phony 反 authentic, genuine　真实的 【考法2】 vt．仿制：to make a fraudulent replica of 例 counterfeit the signature　仿造签名 近 copy, forge, imitate, simulate, mimic
