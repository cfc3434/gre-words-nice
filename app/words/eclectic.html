eclectic [ɪˈklektɪk] 【考法】 adj．混合的，多元化的：composed of elements drawn from various sources 例 The museum's eclectic collection has everything from a giraffe skeleton to medieval musical instruments．博物馆里的展品丰富多样，从长颈鹿的骨架到中世纪的乐器都有。 近 assorted, diverse, heterogeneous, indiscriminate, magpie, mixed, motley, piebald, ragtag, varied 反 homogeneous, uniform　同一性的
