pliable [ˈplaɪəbl] 【考法1】 adj．易弯曲的，柔软的：supple enough to bend freely or repeatedly without breaking 例 pliable optical fiber　柔软的光纤 近 adaptable, ductile, flexible, limber, plastic, supple 反 inflexible, rigid, stiff, unbending　不易弯曲的 【考法2】 adj．易受影响的，温顺的：easily influenced, persuaded, or swayed 例 He took advantage of the pliable mind of youth．他利用了年轻人容易受他人教唆的思想。 近 compliant, docile, obedient, pliant, tractable, subdued 反 contumacious, insubordinate, intractable, obstreperous, recalcitrant, refractory, unruly　难控制的
