satirize [ˈsætəraɪz] 【考法】 vt．讽刺：to ridicule or attack by means of satire 例 The movie satirizes contemporary life．这部电影讽刺当下生活。 派 satire n．讽刺诗，讽刺文学：a literary work holding up human vices and follies to ridicule or scorn 例 a satire about the music industry in which a handsome but untalented youth is turned into a rock star　一部关于一个长得帅却没才华的年轻人如何被打造成一个摇滚巨星的讽刺文学作品 近 lampoon, pasquinade
