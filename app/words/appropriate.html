appropriate 【考法1】 [əˈproʊprieɪt] v．私自挪用：to take possession of or make use of exclusively for oneself, often without permission 例 appropriate private property　盗用私人财产 ‖ The economy has been weakened by corrupt officials who have appropriated the country's resources for their own use．经济因为腐败的官员们私自挪用国家资源而被削弱了。 近 purloin, pirate, embezzle, peculate, usurp 【考法2】 [əˈproʊpriət] adj．适当的：especially suitable or compatible: fitting 例 Red wine is a more appropriate choice with the meal．红酒更适合这顿饭。 近 apt, becoming, felicitous, fitting, proper, meet, suitable, apposite, apropos 反 improper, inapposite, inappropriate, inapt, unmeet, unseemly, unsuitable　不合适的
