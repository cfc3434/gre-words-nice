adversity [ədˈvзːrsəti] 【考法】 n．厄运，逆境：a state, condition, or instance of serious or continued difficulty or adverse fortune 例 The fire is the test of gold, adversity of strong man.（Martha Graham）烈火试真金，逆境炼勇士。——玛莎·葛兰姆 ‖ They finally overcame all the adversities of the Great Depression and rebuilt their fortunes．他们终于度过了大萧条的困境，开始重新创造他们的财富。 近 ill, hardship, misadventure, mischance, mishap, tragedy 反 fortune, fluke, luck, serendipity　好运气
