rehearsal [rɪˈhзːrsl] 【考法】 n．排练，彩排：the act of practicing in preparation for a public performance 例 We made a few mistakes in rehearsal, but we were pretty sure that we'd be OK on opening night．彩排过程中我们犯了几个错误，但正式开幕式当晚我们有信心做得很好。 近 practice, trial, dry run 反 extemporization, impromptu, improvisation（无准备的）即兴表演
