presage [ˈpresɪdʒ] 【考法】 n．征兆：something believed to be a sign or warning of a future event vt．预示，预言：to foretell or predict 例 The sight of the first robin is always a welcome presage of spring．第一只知更鸟的出现总是迎接春天到来的象征。‖ The incident may presage war． 这个事件可能是战争的征兆。 近 augury, auspice, boding, foreboding, foreshadowing, portent, prefiguring; adumbrate, augur, forecast, foretell, portend, predict, prognosticate, prophesy
