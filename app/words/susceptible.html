susceptible [səˈseptəbl] 【考法】 adj．容易受伤害的：being in a situation where one is likely to meet with harm 例 Some people are more susceptible to depression during the winter．一些人更容易在冬天受到沮丧情绪的影响。 近 endangered, exposed, sensitive, subject, vulnerable 反 insusceptible, invulnerable, unexposed, unsusceptible　不受影响的
