genteel [dʒenˈtiːl] 【考法】 adj．有教养的，不粗俗的：free from vulgarity or rudeness 例 Her genteel behaviors at the ball make others conjecture that she must come from a distinguished noble family．舞会上她极有教养的举止让他人纷纷猜测她一定来自名门望族。 近 courteous, decent, decorous, mannerly, polite, polished, respectable, urbane, couth, cultured, refined 反 churlish　粗暴的；loutish　蠢笨的
