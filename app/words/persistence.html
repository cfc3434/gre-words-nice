persistence [pərˈsɪstəns] 【考法】 n．坚持，持续：uninterrupted or lasting existence 例 The persistence of the fever for a week caused me great worry．持续一周的发烧让我很不安。 近 ceaselessness, continuance, continuity, durability, endurance, subsistence 反 cessation, close, end, expiration, finish, stoppage, surcease, termination　停止 派 persistent adj．持续不断的
