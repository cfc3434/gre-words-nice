premeditate [ˌpriːˈmedɪteɪt] 【考法】 vi．预先考虑：to think, consider, or deliberate beforehand 例 carefully premeditate each step of his plan　细致谋略他计划的每一步 近 deliberate, prearrange, prepare, preplan 反 disregard, ignore, neglect, omit, overlook　忽视 派 premeditation n．谋略，事先考虑 【考法】 vt．预谋：to think about and revolve in the mind beforehand 例 He's carefully premeditating each step of his plan of campaign．他正在仔细地计划竞选计划中的每一个步骤。 近 forethink, precogitate, predetermine 反 disregard, ignore, overlook, slight　忽略 派 premeditated adj．有预谋的
