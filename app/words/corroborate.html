corroborate [kəˈrɑːbəreɪt] 【考法1】 vt．用证据或权威证实：to support with evidence or authority; make more certain 例 new evidence to corroborate the defendant's story　能证实被告的故事的新证据 近 confirm, authenticate, justify, substantiate, validate, verify 反 controvert, contradict, deny　反驳，否认 【考法2】 vt．为…提供证据，支持：to provide evidence or information for (as a claim or idea) 例 My personal experience does not corroborate your faith in the essential goodness of people．我的经历可不赞同你关于“人性本善”的信念。 近 back, bolster, buttress, reinforce, support 派 corroboration n．证实
