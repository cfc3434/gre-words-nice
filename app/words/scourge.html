scourge [skзːrdʒ] 【考法】 n．祸害：a source of harm or misfortune 例 The sheer ubiquity of food seems to be the scourge of humanity, as evidenced by the obesity epidemic．无所不在的食品似乎要成为人类的祸害了，这一点可以从肥胖病的泛滥得以佐证。 近 affliction, bane, curse, nemesis 反 benefit, blessing, boon, felicity, godsend, manna, windfall　恩赐，福音
