affinity [əˈfɪnəti] 【考法1】 n．喜欢，倾向：a habitual attraction to some activity or thing 例 She always had an affinity for nurturing living things．她总是喜欢养一些活物。 近 bent, penchant, predilection, predisposition, proclivity, propensity 反 aversion, repugnance, antipathy　厌恶 【考法2】 n．相似：the fact or state of having something in common 例 share obvious affinities　有明显的相似性 近 association, kinship
