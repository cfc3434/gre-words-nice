nirvana [nɪrˈvɑːnə] 【考法】 n．天堂，极乐世界：an often imaginary place or state of utter perfection and happiness 例 They believe in a continuous cycle of births and deaths until the soul is perfected and achieves nirvana．他们相信生命会不断轮回，直到灵魂变得完美并升入天堂。 近 empyrean, fantasyland, heaven, lotusland, utopia 反 hell, inferno　地狱
