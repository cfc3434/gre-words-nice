harmonious [hɑːrˈmoʊniəs] 【考法】 adj．和谐一致的：having the parts agreeably related 例 a harmonious arrangement of archways and doorways in the palace courtyard　宫殿的庭院、拱门和门廊排列有序协调 近 balanced, congruous, consonant, eurythmic, harmonic, accordant, coherent, compatible, concordant, conformable, congruent, consonant, correspondent, nonconflicting 反 disharmonic, disharmonious, incongruous, unbalanced　不协调的；conflicting, incompatible, incongruous, inconsistent　冲突的
