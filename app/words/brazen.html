brazen [ˈbreɪzn] 【考法1】 adj．蛮横大胆的，厚颜无耻的：marked by contemptuous boldness 例 a brazen disregard for the rules　蛮横大胆地漠视规则 近 bold-faced, impertinent, impudent, insolent 反 modest, self-effacing, diffident, retiring, timid　谦虚的，胆小的 【考法2】 v．大胆自信地去面对或从事：to face or undergo with bold self-assurance 例 brazen out the crisis　沉着面对危机 近 confront, outface, defy 反 dodge, duck, shirk, sidestep　躲开，避谈
