catastrophe [kəˈtæstrəfi] 【考法1】 n．大灾难：the final event of the dramatic action especially of a tragedy 近 apocalypse, calamity, cataclysm, debacle, tragedy 【考法2】 n．彻底的失败：utter failure: fiasco 例 The party was a catastrophe．该派对就是一个杯具。 近 bummer, debacle, disaster, fiasco, fizzle, washout 反 blockbuster, hit, smash, success, winner　大胜
