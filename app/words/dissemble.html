dissemble [dɪˈsembl] 【考法】 v．用假象隐藏真相，掩饰：to put on a false appearance 例 dissemble fear with a smile　用微笑来掩饰恐惧 ‖ He dissembled happiness at the news that his ex-girlfriend was getting married to someone else．他听到前女友结婚的消息后假装高兴。 近 dissimulate, affect, assume, bluff, counterfeit, fake, sham 反 behave honestly　表现诚恳
