rustle [ˈrʌsl] 【考法】 vi．快速地行动：to move or act energetically or with speed; to proceed or move quickly 例 The little boy rustled around enthusiastically on the first morning of the trip．旅行的第一个早晨，小男孩充满热情地跑来跑去。 近 bolt, breeze, careen, career, hasten, hustle, jump, run, rush, scurry 反 crawl, creep　匍匐前行；poke　缓慢行动
