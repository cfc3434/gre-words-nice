patina [pəˈtiːnə] 【考法1】 n．外表：a superficial covering or exterior 例 a superficial patina of knowledge　表面肤浅的知识 近 façade, hull, skin, veneer 反 essential quality　本质；core, kernel　核心 【考法2】 n．（由内而外散发的）氛围，气场：an appearance or aura that is derived from association, habit, or established character 例 Although the winery is brand-new, it has been constructed and decorated to give it a patina of old-world quaintness．尽管酿酒厂是全新建造的，但它的构造和装饰都刻意营造出一种古色古香的奇异氛围。 近 air, ambience, aroma, atmosphere, climate, flavor, halo, odor, smell, temper, vibration
