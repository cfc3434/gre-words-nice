pristine [ˈprɪstiːn] 【考法】 adj．纯净的，质朴的，未被文明腐蚀的：remaining in a pure state; uncorrupted by civilization; remaining free from dirt or decay; clean 例 a pristine forest　一片未被文明影响的森林 近 immaculate, spotless, stainless, unsoiled, unstained, unsullied 反 tainted, squalid, contaminated, besmirched, corrupted by civilization　污染的，被文明腐蚀的
