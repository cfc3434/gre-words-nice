exponent [ɪkˈspoʊnənt] 【考法】 n．倡导者，支持者：one that speaks for, represents, or advocates 例 Exponents of space exploration earnestly called for more missions to the outer reaches of the solar system．太空探险的支持者强烈要求对太阳系的外边缘进行探索。 近 advocator, backer, booster, champion, espouser, friend, promoter, proponent 反 adversary, antagonist, opponent　反对者
