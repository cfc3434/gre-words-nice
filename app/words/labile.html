labile [ˈleɪbaɪl] 【考法】 adj．易变的，不稳定的：continually undergoing chemical, physical, or biological change; unstable 例 labile mineral　不稳定的矿物质 ‖ an emotionally labile person　一个多愁善感的人 近 capricious, fluctuating, fluid, inconstant, mercurial, temperamental, unsettled, unsteady, variable, volatile 反 constant, immutable, invariable, stable, stationary, steady　稳定的
