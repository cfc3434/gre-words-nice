barricade [ˌbæriˈkeɪd] 【考法1】 n．障碍物：an obstruction or rampart thrown up across a way or passage 例 The police put up barricades to block off the parade route．警察设立了路障来阻挡游行的队伍。 近 fence, hedge, wall, barrier, obstacle, blockade, obstruction 【考法2】 vt．用障碍物阻止通过：to prevent access to by means of a barricade 例 Streets have been barricaded by authorities．街道被当局封锁了。 近 bar, check, hinder, impede, obstruct, wall off 反 permit　允许
