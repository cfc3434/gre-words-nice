skirt [skзːrt] 【考法】 vt．绕行，避开：to go around or keep away from in order to avoid danger or discovery 例 skirt the construction zone　绕开建筑工地 ‖ The new bill would make it harder for companies to skirt environmental regulations．新的规定会增加公司逃脱环境监管的难度。 近 bypass, circumvent, detour, shortcut, sidestep 反 pursue, seek　追寻
