spat [spæt] 【考法】 n．（小的）争吵：a brief petty quarrel or angry outburst 例 Like any other couple, they have their spats．像其他夫妻一样，他们也会有争执。‖ They were typical sisters, spatting one minute, playing together the next．她们是典型的姐妹：上一分钟还在吵架，下一分钟就又玩在一起了。 近 altercation, controversy, disagreement, dispute, imbroglio, quarrel, squabble, tiff, wrangle 反 amity, concord, harmony, rapport, rapprochement　和睦，友好
