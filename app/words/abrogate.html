abrogate [ˈæbrəɡeɪt] 【考法1】 v．官方的正式废除：to abolish by authoritative action, annul 例 abrogate the law/treaty 废除法律、条约 近 disannul, invalidate, nullify, quash, rescind, roll back, strike down 反 embrace, uphold, institute　采纳；支持；建立 【考法2】 v．无视（某事的）存在：to treat as nonexistent 例 abrogate their responsibilities　抛开、不顾他们的责任
