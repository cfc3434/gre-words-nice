intransigent [ɪnˈtrænzɪdʒənt] 【考法】 adj．不妥协的，固执的：characterized by refusal to compromise or to abandon an extreme position 例 an intransigent attitude　不妥协的态度 近 adamant, headstrong, intractable, obstinate, pertinacious, stubborn, uncompromising, unyielding 反 compliant, pliable　易受影响的；tractable, yielding　容易驾驭的 派 intransigence n．顽固
