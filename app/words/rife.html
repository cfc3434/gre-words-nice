rife [raɪf] 【考法】 adj．丰富的，普遍的：possessing or covered with great numbers or amounts of something specified 例 a video game rife with violence and abuse　充满暴力与虐待的电子游戏 近 abounding, abundant, awash, flush, fraught, lousy, replete, swarming, teeming, thick, thronging 反 deficient, incomplete, insufficient, short　缺乏的
