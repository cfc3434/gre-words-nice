dispatch [dɪˈspætʃ] 【考法1】 n．迅速：promptness and efficiency in performance or transmission 例 do sth with dispatch　迅速地做某事 近 alacrity, haste, swiftness, expedition, promptitude 反 leisureliness, delay, procrastination　悠然；拖延 【考法2】 vt．发送，派遣：to cause to go or be taken from one place to another 例 to dispatch a messenger with urgent news　发送一个紧急消息 近 transfer, transmit, transport, pack off 反 accept, receive　接收
