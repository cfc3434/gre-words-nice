temperate [ˈtempərət] 【考法】 adj．（言行举止）有分寸的：avoiding extremes in behavior or expression adj．有节制的：given to or marked by restraint in the satisfaction of one's appetites 例 He is rather temperate in his appraisal of the movie, calling it good but not great．他对这部电影的赞赏很适度，说其“还好”而不是“很棒”。‖ temperate manner/criticism/language　有分寸的行为/批评/语言 近 abstentious, abstinent, continent, self-abnegating, self-denying, sober 反 immoderate, extreme, frivolous　极端的；self-indulgent　放纵的
