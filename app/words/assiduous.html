assiduous [əˈsɪdʒuəs] 【考法】 adj．勤勉的，专心仔细的：marked by careful unremitting attention or persistent application; busy 例 He always tended his garden with assiduous attention．他总是专心仔细地照看他的花园。‖ The project required some assiduous planning．该项目需要细心的规划。 近 diligent, industrious, sedulous 反 idle, inactive, unbusy, unemployed, unoccupied　空闲的 派 assiduity n．勤勉：persistent application or diligence; unflagging effort
