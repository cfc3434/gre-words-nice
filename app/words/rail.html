rail [reɪl] 【考法】 vi．怒骂，猛烈抨击：to revile or scold in harsh, insolent, or abusive language 例 We could hear the cook in the kitchen railing against his assistant and wondered if we'd ever get our food．我们可以听到厨师在厨房怒斥他的助手，由此我们怀疑我们点的菜还能不能做好。 近 baste, berate, castigate, chastise, hammer, lambaste, rebuke, reprimand, reproach, upbraid 反 accredit, applaud, commend, eulogize, extol, laud, praise　表扬，赞扬 派 raillery n．善意的打闹
