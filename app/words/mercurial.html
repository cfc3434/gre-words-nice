mercurial [mзːrˈkjʊriəl] 【考法】 adj．（情绪）善变的：characterized by rapid and unpredictable changeableness of mood 例 his mercurial temperament　他善变的脾气 近 capricious, changeful, fluctuating, fluid, mutable, temperamental, uncertain, variable, volatile 反 certain, constant, immutable, invariable, settled, stable, steady, unvarying　稳定的，不变的
