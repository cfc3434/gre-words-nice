diffuse 【考法1】 [dɪˈfjuːs] adj．啰嗦的：being at once verbose and ill-organized 例 a diffuse report　冗长的报告 近 circuitous, circumlocutory, long-winded, prolix, verbose, windy, rambling 反 concise, pithy, succinct, terse, laconic　简洁的 【考法2】 [dɪˈfjuːz] v．扩展，散开：to extend, scatter 例 The photographer uses a screen to diffuse the light．摄影师用一个屏幕来分散光线。 近 spread, extend, disperse, disseminate, scatter 反 concentrate, center, centralize, focus　集中
