dabble [ˈdæbl] 【考法】 vi．涉猎，对…浅尝辄止：to work or involve oneself superficially or intermittently especially in a secondaryinterest 例 dabble in arts　对艺术稍有涉猎 反 dedicate, specialize　投入，专门研究 派 dabbler n．涉猎者，浅尝辄止者：one not deeply engaged in or concerned with something 例 He's not a dedicated musician, just a dabbler．他并不是专门的音乐家，只不过是个业余爱好者。 反 specialist　专家
