manumit [ˌmænjuˈmɪt] 【考法】 vt．解放（奴隶）：to release from slavery or bondage 例 Though he was an outspoken defender of liberty, this son of Virginia did not manumit his own slaves until he was on his deathbed．尽管这位维吉尼亚之子公开地支持自由，但他直到临死前才释放自己的奴隶。 近 free, emancipate, enfranchise, liberate, loose, release, unbind, unchain, unshackle 反 detain　拘留；enchain, enfetter, enslave　奴役
