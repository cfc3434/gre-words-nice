chimera [kaɪˈmɪrə] 【考法】 n．虚构的事物，幻觉：an illusion or fabrication of the mind, especially an unrealizable dream 例 EU is just a chimera that can't prevent old nations from playing their dices．欧盟只不过是一个虚构的存在，它根本不可能阻止那些老国家按照自己的一套规则办事。 近 conceit, daydream, delusion, fantasy, figment, hallucination, illusion, phantasm, vision 反 actuality, fact, reality, truth　事实，现实
