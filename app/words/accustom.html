accustom [əˈkʌstəm] 【考法】 vt．使习惯：to make familiar with something through use or experience 例 As a kid from Southern California, the first challenge in his PhD life is to accustom himself to the harsh winter climate in Boston．身为来自南加州的小孩，他博士生涯的第一道挑战就是习惯波士顿寒冷的冬天。 近 familiarize, habituate, inure, orient, wont 反 alienate, estrange, wean　使疏远
