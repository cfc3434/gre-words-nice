penury [ˈpenjəri] 【考法1】 n．贫穷：extreme dearth; barrenness or insufficiency 例 be trapped in penury　被困于贫穷之中 近 destitution, impecuniosity, impoverishment, indigence, neediness, poorness, poverty, want 反 affluence, opulence, richness, wealth　富裕 【考法2】 n．吝啬，节俭：extreme and often niggardly frugality 例 They bitterly complained about their father's penury．他们哀怨地抱怨父亲的节俭。 近 closeness, miserliness, niggardliness, parsimony, penuriousness, stinginess, tightfistedness, tightness 反 generosity, largesse, munificence, openhandedness, philanthropy　慷慨的 派 penurious adj．贫穷的；吝啬的
