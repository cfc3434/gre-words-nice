contempt [kənˈtempt] 【考法】 n．蔑视，鄙视：open dislike for someone or something considered unworthy of one's concern or respect 例 my undying contempt for people who abuse animals　我对于虐待动物者的无尽鄙视 ‖ Her contempt for illiterate was obvious．你可以很明显地看出她对文盲的鄙夷。 近 contemptuousness, despisement, despite, despitefulness, disdain, misprision, scorn 反 admiration, esteem, estimation, regard, respect, reverence, veneration　尊敬，敬仰 派 contemptible adj．可鄙的；contemptuous adj．蔑视的
