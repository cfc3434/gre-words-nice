ignominy [ˈɪɡnəmɪni] 【考法】 n．耻辱：the state of having lost the esteem of others 例 He spent the remainder of his life in ignominy after being involved in a bribery scandal．因为卷入了受贿丑闻当中，他在耻辱中度过余生。 近 discredit, disesteem, dishonor, disrepute, infamy, obloquy, odium, opprobrium, reproach, shame 反 glory, honor　荣耀；esteem, respect　敬意 派 ignominious adj．可鄙的
