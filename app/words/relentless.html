relentless [rɪˈlentləs] 【考法1】 adj．残酷的，无情的：showing or promising no abatement of severity, intensity, strength, or pace 例 relentless pressure　无情的压力 近 grim, inexorable, ironfisted, merciless, mortal, ruthless, unrelenting 反 charitable, clement, merciful, lenient　仁慈的 【考法2】 adj．固执的，不肯妥协的：showing no signs of slackening or yielding in one's purpose 例 The team's offense was relentless in trying to score．进攻球员毫不妥协地努力得分。 近 adamant, dogged, headstrong, mulish, obdurate, pertinacious, rigid, uncompromising, unyielding 反 acquiescent, amenable, compliant, complying, flexible, pliable, pliant, relenting, yielding　易屈服的
