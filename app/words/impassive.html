impassive [ɪmˈpæsɪv] 【考法】 adj．冷漠的，无感情的：giving no sign of feeling or emotion 例 She remained impassive as the officers informed her of her son's death．她面不改色地听着警官宣读她儿子的死讯。 近 affectless, apathetic, cold-blooded, deadpan, emotionless, numb, phlegmatic, stoic, undemonstrative 反 demonstrative, emotional, fervent, fervid, impassioned, passionate, vehement　充满感情的
