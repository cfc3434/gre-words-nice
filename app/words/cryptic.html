cryptic [ˈkrɪptɪk] 【考法1】 adj．秘密的：secret or occult 例 cryptic message　秘密的信息 近 covert, furtive, secret, stealthy 反 public, open　公开的 【考法2】 adj．难以理解的：being beyond one's powers to know, understand, or explain 例 They were puzzled by the cryptic e-mail message left on his computer．他们被他电脑里让人费解的电子邮件所迷惑。 近 arcane, enigmatic, impenetrable, inscrutable, mystic, occult, uncanny 反 comprehensible, understandable　可以理解的
