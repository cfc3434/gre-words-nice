contagious [kənˈteɪdʒəs] 【考法1】 adj．传染的：communicable by contact; catching 例 contagious diseases　传染病 近 infectious, pestilent, transmissible, epidemic 反 incommunicable　不传染的 派 contagiousness n．可传染性 【考法2】 adj．（情绪等）唤起共鸣的：exciting a similar feeling or reaction in others 例 The enthusiasm of the new club members was contagious．新成员的热情具有很强的感染力。 近 catching, spreading
