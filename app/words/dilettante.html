dilettante [ˌdɪləˈtænti] 【考法】 n./adj．业余爱好者（对艺术或知识领域涉猎浅薄者）/缺乏专业技术的：a person having a superficial interest in an art/lacking or showing a lack of expert skill 例 Dilettante watch the scene of bustle, adept guard the entrance．外行看热闹，内行看门道。 近 dabbler, amateur, nonexpert, cognoscente; nonprofessional, amateur 反 authority, expert, pro, professional, specialist　专家
