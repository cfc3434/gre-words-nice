limp [lɪmp] 【考法1】 adj．柔软的，松散的：lacking firm texture, substance, or structure 例 Her hair hung limp about her shoulders．她的头发松散地垂在肩上。 近 droopy, flaccid, floppy, lank, yielding 反 firm, stiff, sturdy, tense　坚硬的；resilient　有弹性的 【考法2】 vi．跛行，艰难地行走：to move or proceed haltingly or unsteadily 例 The project limped along with half its previous funding．项目靠着之前一半的资金艰难地进行着。 近 blunder, bumble, lumber, plod, struggle, stumble, trudge
