vitality [vaɪˈtæləti] 【考法】 n．活力，生命力：physical or mental vigor especially when highly developed 例 lose intellectual vitality　失去思维活力 近 animation, bounce, dynamism, energy, liveliness, robustness, verve, vibrancy, vigorousness, vim 反 lethargy, listlessness, sluggishness, torpidity　精神不振 派 vitalize vt．激发，使有活力
