sacrilege [ˈsækrəlɪdʒ] 【考法】 n．亵渎圣物：desecration, profanation, misuse, or theft of something sacred 例 It is a sacrilege to offend democracy．这是一种对民主的亵渎。 近 blasphemy, defilement, desecration, irreverence, impiety, profanation, violation 反 adoration, glorification, respect, reverence　爱慕，尊敬 派 sacrilegious adj．不敬的，亵渎神明的
