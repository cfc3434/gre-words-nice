wane [weɪn] 【考法】 vi．减少，衰退，降低：to decrease in size, extent, or degree 例 In the evening the storm finally waned．傍晚时分风暴终于减弱了。‖ The moon waxes and then wanes．月有阴晴圆缺。 近 abate, decline, diminish, dwindle, ease, ebb, fall, lessen, lower, moderate, recede, shrink, subside, taper 反 accumulate, balloon, burgeon, enlarge, escalate, expand, grow, increase, intensify, mushroom, rise, snowball, soar, wax　增加，扩大，上升 派 waning adj．衰退的，减少的
