dodge [dɑːdʒ] 【考法】 v．躲避：to avoid (a blow, for example) by moving or shifting quickly aside 例 dodge a storm of bullets　躲避枪林弹雨 ‖ He dodged the first punch but was hit by the second．他躲过了第一次打击，但是没有躲过第二次。 近 avoid, escape, shirk, malinger, goldbrick, evade, parry, sidestep, circumvent, fence, hedge, avert, elude, shun, skirt, bilk, eschew, weasel
