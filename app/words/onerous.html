onerous [ˈɑːnərəs] 【考法】 adj．费时间花心思的：requiring much time, effort, or careful attention 例 Building the scale model of the frigate was an onerous task．建造军舰的小模型非常花时间。 近 arduous, burdensome, challenging, exacting, grueling, taxing, toilsome 反 requiring little effort, light, nondemanding, unchallenging, undemanding　轻松的，不费劲的
