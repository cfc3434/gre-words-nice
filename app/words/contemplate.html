contemplate [ˈkɑːntəmpleɪt] 【考法】 v．沉思，仔细思索：to view or consider with continued attention 例 contemplate the vastness of the universe　沉思着宇宙的广袤无垠 ‖ She contemplated the problem for several hours before reaching a decision．关于这个问题，她苦苦思索了几个小时后才做出决定。 近 cogitate, consider, deliberate, meditate, mull, perpend, pore, revolve, ruminate, study, weigh, wrestle 反 disregard, ignore, overlook, slight　忽视 派 contemplation n．沉思，思索
