aspersion [əˈspзːrʒn] 【考法】 n．诽谤，中伤：a false or misleading charge meant to harm someone's reputation 例 cast aspersions on my loyalty　诽谤我的忠诚 近 defamation, besmirchment, calumny, calumniation, maligning, obloquy, vilification 反 glowing tribute, eulogy, extolling, laudation, praise, commendation, compliment　热情赞赏，表扬；flattery, adulation　拍马屁
