grumble [ˈɡrʌmbl] 【考法】 vi．（尤指低声地）抱怨，埋怨：to complain in a surly manner; mutter discontentedly 例 The governed will always find something to grumble about.（Crane Brinton）被统治者总能找到发牢骚的理由。——克莱恩·布林顿 近 carp, croak, fuss, gripe, grouch, grouse, grump, moan, murmur, mutter, repine 反 crow, delight, rejoice　感到高兴
