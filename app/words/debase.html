debase [dɪˈbeɪs] 【考法】 v．贬低，贬损：to reduce to a lower standing in one's own eyes or in others'eyes 例 Our failure to win a single game completely debased our image．我们一场未胜的战绩彻底让我们的形象一落千丈。 近 abase, debauch, degrade, demean, demoralize, deprave, deteriorate, profane, subvert, vitiate 反 aggrandize, canonize, deify, elevate, exalt, ennoble　使神圣
