phlegmatic [fleɡˈmætɪk] 【考法】 adj．冷静的，无感情的，淡漠的：having or suggesting a calm, sluggish temperament; unemotional 例 a strangely phlegmatic response to what should have been happy news　对本该是一个让人开心的新闻的冷漠回应 近 affectless, apathetic, cold-blooded, emotionless, impassible, numb, stoic, stolid 反 demonstrative, emotional, fervent, fervid, impassioned, passionate, vehement　感情丰富的，热情的
