warp [wɔːrp] 【考法】 vt．曲解：to change so much as to create a wrong impression or alter the meaning of 例 The faulty English translation really warps the meaning of the original Chinese text．错误的英语翻译曲解了中文的原意。 近 distort, falsify, misinterpret, misrepresent, twist 反 clarify, clear, explain, illuminate, illustrate　澄清
