recant [rɪˈkænt] 【考法】 vt．撤回，放弃，改变：to withdraw or repudiate (a statement or belief) formally and publicly 例 The man has refused after torture to recant his heresy．这个人在受折磨后依旧拒绝放弃他的异教信仰。 近 abjure, abnegate, forswear, renege, renounce, repeal, repudiate, retract, withdraw 反 adhere, insist, maintain, stick　坚持 派 recantation n．改变论调
