appreciable [əˈpriːʃəbl] 【考法】 adj．可感知的，明显的：capable of being perceived or measured; perceptible 例 the appreciable changes in temperature　气温明显的变化 ‖ There doesn't seem to be any appreciable difference between this piece and that one．这个和那个看上去没什么明显的差别。 近 apprehensible, perceptible, detectable, discernible, palpable, distinguishable, sensible 反 impalpable, imperceptible, inappreciable, indistinguishable, insensible, undetectable　不能感知的
