proclaim [prəˈkleɪm] 【考法】 vt．宣告，使…公之于众：to declare publicly, typically insistently, proudly, or defiantly and in either speech or writing 例 He launched a coup and proclaimed himself President．他发动了一场政变，然后宣称自己已成为总统。 近 advertise, announce, blare, blazon, broadcast, declare, enunciate, herald, promulgate, publicize, publish, release, trumpet 反 conceal, hide　隐藏；silence, suppress　压制（观点的发表）
