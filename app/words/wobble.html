wobble [ˈwɑːbl] 【考法1】 vi．摇晃，颤抖：to move or proceed with an irregular rocking or staggering motion or unsteadily and clumsily from side to side 例 The drunk stood up, wobbled for a moment, and fell forward．醉汉站了起来，晃动了一阵，然后向前倒了下去。 近 rock, totter 反 stabilize　使稳定 【考法2】 vi．犹豫不决：to show uncertainty about the right course of action 例 We cannot tolerate the government wobbling at this critical time．我们不能容忍政府在这种关键时刻犹豫不决。 近 falter, stagger, teeter, vacillate, waver
