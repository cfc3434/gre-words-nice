retrograde [ˈretrəɡreɪd] 【考法】 adj．倒退的：moving or tending backward vi．退步，退化：to decline to a worse condition 例 This is a retrograde step and you will regret it．这是后退的一步，你以后肯定会后悔的。‖ The Dark Ages is the period following the fall of the Roman Empire when Western civilization seriously retro-graded.“黑暗时代”指的是罗马帝国崩溃之后，西方文明严重退步的一个历史时期。 近 backward, receding, regressive, reversed, withdrawing; atrophy, crumble, decay, decline, degenerate, descend, deteriorate, ebb, regress, rot, sink, worsen 反 progressive　前进的；ameliorate, improve, meliorate　改善
