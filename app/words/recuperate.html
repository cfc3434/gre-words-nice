recuperate [rɪˈkuːpəreɪt] 【考法】 v．恢复（健康或力量），康复：to recover health or strength 例 He is gradually recuperating from a serious back injury．他正渐渐地从严重的背部损伤中恢复过来。 近 convalesce, heal, rally, recoup, recover, rehabilitate 反 deteriorate, flag, wane, weaken　衰退，变得衰弱；die, expire, perish, pass away　死亡 派 recuperative adj．有助于康复的
