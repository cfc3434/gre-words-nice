lofty [ˈlɔːfti] 【考法1】 adj．崇高的：elevated in character and spirit, noble 例 lofty ideals　崇高的理想 近 chivalrous, elevated, greathearted, high-minded, magnanimous, sublime 反 base, debased, ignominious, mean　可耻的 【考法2】 adj．自大的：having a feeling of superiority that shows itself in an overbearing attitude 例 lofty distain　自大的不满 近 assumptive, bumptious, haughty, lordly, peremptory, pompous, presumptuous, supercilious, superior 反 humble, lowly, modest　谦逊的，低调的
