methodical [məˈθɑːdɪkl] 【考法】 adj．井然有序的，有条理的：arranged or proceeding in regular, systematic order 例 a methodical summary that included lists of points to memorize　一份包含了记忆重点的有条理的总结 近 neat, orderly, organized, regular, systematic, systematized 反 disorganized, haphazard, irregular, unsystematic　杂乱的，混乱无序的
