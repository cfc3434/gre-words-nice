gregarious [ɡrɪˈɡeriəs] 【考法1】 adj．爱社交的：likely to seek or enjoy the company of others 近 convivial, extroverted, genial, outgoing, sociable, outgoing 反 aloof　疏远的；antisocial　不合群的；introverted, reclusive　内向的 【考法2】 adj．群居的：tending to group with others of the same kind 例 ecologically define human as gregarious carnivore　从生态学上将人类定义为群居类肉食动物 近 social 反 solitary　独自的
