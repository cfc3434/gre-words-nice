hauteur [hɔːˈtзːr] 【考法】 n．傲慢，自大：haughtiness in bearing and attitude; arrogance 例 behave with hauteur　举止傲慢 近 bumptiousness, imperiousness, peremptoriness, pomposity, presumptuousness, pretentiousness, super-ciliousness, superiority 反 humility, humbleness, modesty, unassumingness, unpretentiousness　谦虚
