superficial [ˌsuːpərˈfɪʃl] 【考法】 adj．表面的，肤浅的：lacking in depth, solidity, and comprehensiveness 例 The official's superficial report on the situation provoked wide blame on the Internet．官员对于情况的敷衍陈述激起了网络上的广泛谴责。 近 cursory, facile, perfunctory, shallow, sketchy, skin-deep 反 profound　深刻的；comprehensive, exhaustive　全面的 派 superficiality n．表面现象
