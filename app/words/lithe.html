lithe [laɪð] 【考法1】 adj．敏捷的，轻盈的：characterized by easy flexibility and grace 例 lithe dancers　轻盈优雅的舞者 近 agile, featly, feline, gracile, lightsome, nimble 反 awkward, clumsy, graceless, ungainly　笨拙的 【考法2】 adj．柔软的：easily bent or flexed 例 lithe branches　柔软的枝条 近 flexible, limber, pliable, pliant, supple 反 solid　坚硬的；inflexible, rigid, stiff, stiffened　僵硬的
