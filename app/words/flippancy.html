flippancy [ˈflɪpənsi] 【考法】 n．轻率，无礼：unbecoming levity or pertness especially in respect to grave or sacred matters 例 The flippancy of your answer annoyed me．你轻率的回答让我很恼火。 近 facetiousness, flightiness, frivolousness, frothiness, levity, silliness 反 earnestness, gravity, seriousness, soberness, solemnity, solemnness　严肃，认真
