fracture [ˈfræktʃər] 【考法】 n．破裂：the act or process of breaking vt．打碎，破坏：to cause to separate into pieces usually suddenly or forcibly 例 a sudden fracture of the established order　已建立起的秩序被突然打破 ‖ Their happiness was fractured by an unforeseen tragedy．他们的幸福生活被突如其来的悲剧打破了。 近 breach, break, rupture, schism, split; disrupt, fragment, rive 反 unity　统一；cement　接合
