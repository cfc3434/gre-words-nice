immanent [ˈɪmənənt] 【考法】 adj．内在的：being a part of the innermost nature of a person or thing 例 Beauty is not something imposed but something immanent．美丽是内在的品格而非外加的东西。 近 constitutional, essential, inborn, inbred, indigenous, ingrained, innate, integral, intrinsic, natural 反 adventitious, extrinsic, extraneous　外在的，非本源的
