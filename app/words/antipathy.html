antipathy [ænˈtɪpəθi] 【考法】 n．厌恶，反感：settled aversion or dislike 例 I feel no antipathy towards any of my opponents in the tournament．我对锦标赛中碰到的任何对手都不存在反感。 近 animosity, animus, antagonism, bitterness, enmity, gall, grudge, hostility, jaundice, rancor 反 amity　友好，和睦；liking, partiality, predilection, prepossession　偏爱，喜爱
