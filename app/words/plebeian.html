plebeian [pləˈbiːən] 【考法】 adj．平民的，社会下层的：belonging to the class of people of low social or economic rank 例 a man who rose to greatness but never forgot his plebeian past　一个仕途通达、但从未忘却自己平民出身的人 近 baseborn, common, humble, inferior, lowborn, lumpen, mean, prole, proletarian, unwashed, vulgar 反 aristocratic, genteel, gentle, highborn, highbred, lofty, noble, patrician, wellborn　上流社会的
