varnish [ˈvɑːrnɪʃ] 【考法】 vt．粉饰（令人不悦的东西）：to cover or conceal (as something unpleasant) with something that gives an attractive appearance 例 She tried to varnish her mistakes．她试图掩盖她的失误。 近 blanch, extenuate, gloss, palliate, sugarcoat, veneer 反 bare, disclose, discover, divulge, expose, reveal, uncloak, uncover, unmask, unveil　揭露
