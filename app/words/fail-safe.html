fail-safe [feɪl-seɪf] 【考法1】 n．保险措施：a measure taken to preclude loss or injury 例 There are so many fail-safes built into the system that a highly unlikely series of mistakes would have to be made before failure could occur．系统中嵌入了大量的保险措施，因此在系统崩溃之前，必须要发生一系列几乎不可能出现的错误。 近 caution, palladium, preventive, safeguard 【考法2】 adj．万无一失的：having no chance of failure 例 a fail-safe device　自动防故障装置 ‖ Men have traditionally regarded flowers as the fail-safe gift for Valentine's Day．男士们长期以来认为鲜花是情人节永远不会错的礼物。 近 certain, foolproof, sure, unfailing 反 fallible　容易犯错的
