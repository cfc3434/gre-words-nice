slovenly [ˈslʌvnli] 【考法】 adj．邋遢的，不整洁的：lacking neatness in dress or person 例 For the sake of their image, the band members transformed themselves from clean-cut lads to slovenly rockers．为了他们自身的形象，乐队成员从健康向上的青年变成了邋遢的摇滚乐手。 近 blowsy, dowdy, frowsy, sloppy, unkempt, untidy 反 dapper, dashing, neat, sharp, smart, spruce, trim　外表整洁的
