consent [kənˈsent] 【考法】 n．同意，赞同：the approval by someone in authority for the doing of something vi．同意：to give assent or approval 例 We had to get our neighbor's consent in order to trim the tree from his side．想要从邻居家那边修剪树木，我们得先得到他的同意。‖ consent to being tested in her neurobiology experiment　同意在她神经生物学的实验中作测试对象 ‖ refuse to consent to the marriage　拒绝这桩婚事 近 allowance, approval, authorization, clearance, concurrence, granting, license, sanction, warrant; acquiesce, agree, approve, assent, subscribe 反 interdiction, prohibition, proscription　禁止；dissent　反对；deny, veto　否决
