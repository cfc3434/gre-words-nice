flush [flʌʃ] 【考法1】 adj．大量的：marked by abundance 例 a field flush with flowers　长满了鲜花的原野 近 abounding, abundant, awash, fraught, lousy, replete, swarming, teeming, thronging 反 inadequate, insufficient, scant, scarce, short　匮乏的，不足的 【考法2】 vi．冲洗：to pour liquid over or through in order to cleanse 例 flush a wound with iodine　用碘酒冲洗伤口 近 irrigate, rinse, sluice, wash
