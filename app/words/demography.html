demography [dɪˈmɑːɡrəfi] 【考法】 n．人口统计学：the study of changes (such as the number of births, deaths, marriages, and illnesses) that occur over a period of time in human populations 例 Even if you look at countries in the same income bracket, there are plenty of factors, from demography to culture, that confuse the picture．即使观察处在相同收入等级的国家，也有众多的因素如从人口分布到文化差异使情况变得复杂。
