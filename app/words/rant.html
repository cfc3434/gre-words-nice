rant [rænt] 【考法】 n．（尤指长时间的）训斥，责骂：a long angry speech or scolding 例 After complaining about the hotel's lousy service, the woman went off on another rant about the condition of her room．在抱怨完旅店差劲的服务之后，她继续对房间的条件开骂。 近 castigation, diatribe, harangue, lambasting, philippic, reprimand, reproach, vituperation 反 encomium, eulogy, panegyric, rhapsody, tribute　赞美之词
