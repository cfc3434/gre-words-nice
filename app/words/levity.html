levity [ˈlevəti] 【考法】 n．轻浮：excessive or unseemly frivolity 例 The teachers disapprove of any displays of levity during school assemblies．老师们不允许学生在学校聚会中表现出任何轻浮的态度。 近 facetiousness, flightiness, flippancy, frivolousness, frothiness, silliness 反 earnestness, gravity, seriousness, soberness, solemnity　严肃
