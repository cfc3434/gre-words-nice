vignette [vɪnˈjet] 【考法】 n．简介，短文：a vivid representation in words of someone or something 例 The general's memoirs are filled with revealing vignettes of some of the war's most compelling personalities．将军的回忆录里充满了短小的文章，它们揭露了战争中最引人注意的人物。 近 definition, delineation, depiction, picture, portrait, portraiture, portrayal, rendering, sketch
