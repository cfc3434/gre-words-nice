attest [əˈtest] 【考法】 vt．证实，为…作证：to give evidence or testimony to the truth or factualness of 例 Her fine work attests her ability.　她的优秀作品能够证明她的能力。 近 argue, authenticate, certify, corroborate, substantiate, support, testify, validate, verify, vindicate 反 contradict, disprove, gainsay, rebut, refute　反驳，否定
