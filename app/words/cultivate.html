cultivate [ˈkʌltɪveɪt] 【考法1】 vt．提升，加强：to improve by labor, care, or study 例 cultivate the mind　开化心智 近 educate, instruct, illuminate, nurse 反 degrade, deteriorate, impair　损坏，降低品质 【考法2】 vt．种植，培养：to promote the growth of (a biological culture) 例 cultivate vegetables　种植蔬菜 近 grow, breed, produce, raise, develop, nurture 反 balk, frustrate, thwart　阻碍 派 cultivation n．栽培
