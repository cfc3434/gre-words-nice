maverick [ˈmævərɪk] 【考法】 n．特立独行之人：a person who does not conform to generally accepted standards or customs adj．标新立异的，不合常规的：deviating from commonly accepted beliefs or practices 例 a maverick view on marriage　关于婚姻的非传统看法 近 bohemian, deviant, heretic, iconoclast, nonconformist; dissentient, dissenting, dissident, heterodox, iconoclastic, unorthodox 反 conformer, conformist, conventionalist　遵从传统的人；conforming, conventional, orthodox　遵从传统的
