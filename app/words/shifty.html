shifty [ˈʃɪfti] 【考法】 adj．（显得）狡诈的，（显得）欺诈的：having, displaying, or suggestive of deceitful character 例 shifty practices such as turning back the odometers on used cars　一些欺骗性的花招，比如把旧车的里程表调零 ‖ He had a shifty face so I won't trust him．他看起来贼眉鼠眼的，我不会相信他。 近 crooked, deceitful, deceptive, duplicitous, fraudulent, guileful, rogue, shady, sharp, underhanded 反 aboveboard, honest, straight　正派的，光明正大的
