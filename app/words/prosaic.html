prosaic [prəˈzeɪɪk] 【考法】 adj．单调的，常见的：being of the type that is encountered in the normal course of events 例 prosaic advice　老掉牙的建议 近 commonplace, everyday, routine, unexceptional, unremarkable, workaday 反 abnormal, exceptional, extraordinary, odd, out-of-the-way, strange, exciting, preternatural, ingenious, imaginary　不寻常的，有独创性的
