defray [dɪˈfreɪ] 【考法】 vt．支付：to undertake the payment of 例 The government has committed billions toward defraying the costs of the war．政府为战争付出了数十亿元的代价。‖ I don't have sufficient fund to defray the expense．我身上的现金不够买单。 近 disburse, expend, pay 反 acquire, earn, gain, garner, procure, realize, secure, win　获得，获取；charge　收取
