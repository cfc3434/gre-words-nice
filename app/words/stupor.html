stupor [ˈstuːpər] 【考法】 n．迟钝，麻痹，无知觉：a condition of greatly dulled or completely suspended sense or sensibility 例 The heartbreaking man lapsed into an alcoholic stupor．那个伤心的男人陷入了无知觉的酒醉状态。 近 coma, dullness, languor, lethargy, lassitude, listlessness, torpidity, torpor 反 alertness, vigilance　警惕，警戒；vigor, vim, vitality, vivacity　活力，精力
