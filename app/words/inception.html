inception [ɪnˈsepʃn] 【考法】 n．开端，开始：an act, process, or instance of beginning 例 This seemed like a good program at its inception, but it isn't working out as planned．一开始这像是个不错的项目，但是它没有按照我们的预期发展。 近 beginning, birth, commencement, dawn, genesis, kickoff, launch, nascence, onset, outset, start, threshold 反 close, conclusion, end, termination, omega　结束，终止 派 inceptive adj．开端的，初生的
