canny [ˈkæni] 【考法】 adj．精明的，聪明的：careful and shrewd, especially where one's own interests are concerned 例 He is a canny card player who is good at psyching out his opponents．他是一个善于琢磨对手心思的精明的扑克玩家。 近 astute, clever, hardheaded, heady, knowing, savvy, sharp, shrewd, smart 反 ignorant, unknowing　无知的；foolish, idiotic, imbecile, moronic, silly, thoughtless, witless　愚笨的
