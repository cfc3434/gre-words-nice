demagogue [ˈdeməɡɑːɡ] 【考法】 n．蛊惑民心的政客：a leader who makes use of popular prejudices and false claims and promises in order to gain power 例 That politician is just a demagogue who preys upon people's fears and prejudices．那就是一个蛊惑民心的政客，利用民众的害怕和偏见来欺骗他们。 近 firebrand, fomenter, incendiary, instigator, kindler, provocateur
