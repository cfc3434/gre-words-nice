authority [əˈθɔːrəti] 【考法1】 n．（某领域的）权威人士：a person with a high level of knowledge or skill in a field 例 a leading authority on neural anatomy　一名神经解剖学领域的权威人士 近 expert, connoisseur, maestro, master, virtuoso 反 amateur　业余爱好者；inexpert　非专家 【考法2】 n．管辖权，控制：lawful control over the affairs of a political unit (as a nation) 例 The sheriff had authority over the whole county．郡守对于整个郡都有管辖权。 近 administration, governance, regime, regimen
