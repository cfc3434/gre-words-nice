persevere [ˌpзːrsəˈvɪr] 【考法】 vi．坚持不懈，不屈不挠：to persist in or remain constant to a purpose, idea, or task in the face of obstacles or discouragement 例 Although he was frustrated by the lack of financial resources and support, he persevered in his scientific research．尽管因为资金不足而受阻，但他仍坚持进行科学研究。 近 carry on, endure, persist 反 renounce, succumb, surrender, yield　放弃，屈服 派 perseverance n．坚持不懈
