renege [rɪˈniːɡ] 【考法】 vt．放弃，摒弃：to solemnly or formally reject or go back on (as something formerly adhered to) 例 She refused to renege the principles by which she had always lived her life, even if it resulted in losing her business．尽管有可能使她失去她的事业，但她仍然拒绝放弃生命中所坚持的那些原则。 近 abnegate, forswear, recant, renounce, repeal, repudiate, retract, withdraw 反 adhere　坚持 派 renegade n．叛徒
