slur [slзːr] 【考法1】 n．耻辱：a mark of guilt or disgrace 例 Your drunken behavior at the wedding has cast a slur on this family．你在婚礼上醉酒后的行为给整个家族都带来了耻辱。 近 blot, brand, onus, smirch, smudge, spot, stigma, taint 反 award, credit, glory, honor　荣誉，荣耀 【考法2】 vt．疏忽，忽略：to slide or slip over without due mention, consideration, or emphasis 例 This documentary slurs over certain important facts as it offers a very biased case for a conspiracy theory．这部纪录片忽略了许多重要的客观事实，而只给出了带有偏见的阴谋论解释。 近 bypass, disregard, forget, ignore, overlook, overpass, slight 反 attend, heed, mind　留心，注意
