ethereal [iˈθɪriəl] 【考法1】 adj．轻巧精致的：resembling air in lightness, highly refined; delicate 例 The bakery's scrumptious pastries have a wonderfully ethereal consistency．面包师可口的糕点有着一种奇妙的蓬松轻巧的质感。 近 fluffy, gossamer, light 反 heavy, leaden, ponderous　沉重的 【考法2】 adj．非物质的，精神（上）的：not of this world; spiritual; not composed of matter 例 that ethereal attribute that every performer should have—charisma　每位演奏者都应该具有一种精神上的特质——个人魅力 近 bodiless, formless, incorporeal, insubstantial, nonmaterial, nonphysical, spiritual 反 bodily, corporeal, material, physical, substantial　物质的，有实体的
