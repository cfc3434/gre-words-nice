affectation [ˌæfekˈteɪʃn] 【考法】 n．虚伪，做作：the act of taking on or displaying an attitude or mode of behavior not natural to oneself or not genuinely felt 例 His little affectations irritated her.　他做作的小动作激怒了她。 近 façade, guise, mannerism, pose, pretense, show 反 artlessness, genuineness, innocence, naivety　天真，淳朴
