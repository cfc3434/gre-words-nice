invoke [ɪnˈvoʊk] 【考法1】 vt．实施：to put into effect or operation 例 New train timetable has been invoked．新的火车时刻表已经生效。 近 enforce, effect, execute, implement, perform 反 suspend　暂停，搁置 【考法2】 vt．产生，造成：to be the cause of (a situation, action, or state of mind) 例 We should be prepared for the possibility that any solution may invoke another set of problems．我们必须要为这样一种可能做好心理准备：任何解决方案都可能带来一系列新的问题。 近 beget, bring, catalyze, cause, create, engender, generate, induce, produce, result
