anemic [əˈniːmɪk] 【考法】 adj．缺乏力量、活力、精神的：lacking force, vitality, or spirit 例 an anemic economic recovery　毫无活力的经济复苏 ‖ Investors are worried about the stock's anemic performance．投资者担心股市低迷的表现。 近 sapless, infirm, feeble, decrepit, wan, pale, pallid, effete, lethargic 反 vigorous, spirited　精力充沛的；forceful　有力量的
