remonstrance [rɪˈmɑːnstrəns] 【考法】 n．抗议，抱怨：an expression of protest, complaint, or reproof, especially a formal statement of grievances 例 She seems deaf to her son's remonstrances．她似乎对儿子的抗议充耳不闻。‖ Aggravated by the noisy metro construction, many residents wrote letters of remonstrance to city officials．许多被地铁建设的噪音激怒的居民向市政府投去了抗议信。 近 challenge, complaint, demur, difficulty, fuss, objection, protest, question, remonstration, stink 反 acceptance, acquiescence, agreement, approval, assent, sanction　同意，接受
