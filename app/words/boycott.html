boycott [ˈbɔɪkɑːt] 【考法】 vt．联合抵制，拒绝参与：to engage in a concerted refusal to have dealings with (as a person, store, or organization) usually to express disapproval or to force acceptance of certain conditions 例 This brand is being boycotted for damaging environment．因为破坏环境，这个品牌正在被抵制。 近 refuse 反 patronize　经常光顾
