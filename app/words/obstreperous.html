obstreperous [əbˈstrepərəs] 【考法】 adj．不听话的，任性的：stubbornly resistant to control; unruly; given to resisting authority or another's control 例 The club's president was at his wits' end with obstreperous members who refused to cooperate．俱乐部主席拿那些不合作的会员一点办法都没有。 近 balky, defiant, incompliant, insubordinate, intractable, rebellious, recalcitrant, refractory 反 disciplined, amenable, biddable, compliant, conformable, docile, obedient, ruly, submissive, tractable　守纪律的，顺从的
