clot [klɑːt] 【考法1】 n．密集的一群：a number of things considered as a unit 例 A clot of daisies occupied one corner of the flower bed．一簇雏菊占据了花床的一角。 近 array, assemblage, band, block, bunch, cluster, clutch, collection, constellation, grouping, huddle, knot, lot, muster, package 【考法2】 v．凝结：to turn from a liquid into a substance resembling jelly 例 clotted blood　凝结的血块 近 congeal, jell, jelly
