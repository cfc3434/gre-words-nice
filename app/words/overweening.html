overweening [ˌoʊvərˈwiːnɪŋ] 【考法】 adj．傲慢的，专横的：having a feeling of superiority that shows itself in an overbearing attitude 例 His modesty is just a masquerade for his overweening conceit．他的谦虚只不过是掩盖其傲慢本质的面具。 近 assumptive, arrogant, bumptious, haughty, imperious, peremptory, pompous, presuming, presumptuous, pretentious, supercilious 反 humble, modest　谦逊的
