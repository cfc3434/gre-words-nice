languor [ˈlæŋɡər] 【考法1】 n．懒惰：physical or mental inertness 例 He enjoyed the languor brought on by a hot summer afternoon．他很享受夏日午后的慵懒。 近 collapse, exhaustion, frazzle, lassitude, listlessness, stupor, torpor, prostration 反 verve, vim, animation, vitality　有活力 【考法2】 n．衰弱：weakness or weariness of body or mind 例 The tropical heat sapped our strength, leaving us in a state of unaccustomed languor．热带的炎热气候消磨着我们的精力，让我们感到一阵不适的虚弱。 近 debilitation, enervation, enfeeblement, fragility, infirmity 反 robustness, strength, vivacity　强壮，有力
