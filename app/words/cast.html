cast [kæst] 【考法1】 vt．提出：to put forth, give off, to place as if by throwing 例 cast doubt on their reliability　对它们的可靠性提出质疑 近 discharge, emanate, irradiate, issue, shoot, throw out, give out 【考法2】 v．抛弃：to get rid of as useless or unwanted 例 Once she became rich and didn't need them anymore, she cast off all her old friends like so much junk．当她有钱了不再需要朋友了，她把老友像糟粕一样抛弃了。 近 ditch, dump, jettison, toss
