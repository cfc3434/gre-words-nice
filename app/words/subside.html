subside [səbˈsaɪd] 【考法】 vi．下陷，下沉，减弱：to tend downward 例 Local officials say the flood waters have subsided．当地官员表示洪水已经退去。‖ As the noise of the siren subsided, I was able to fall back to sleep．随着警笛声的减弱，我可以再次睡着了。‖ The pain will subside in a couple of hours．几个小时之内疼痛感就会退去。 近 abate, decline, decrease, diminish, drop, dwindle, ebb, fall, lessen, moderate, recede, wane 反 balloon, burgeon, escalate, expand, grow, increase, intensify, rise, snowball, soar, wax　上升，增大 派 subsidence n．下沉
