awe [ɔː] 【考法】 n./v．敬畏：an emotion variously combining dread, veneration, and wonder that is inspired by authority or by the sacred or sublime 例 She gazed in awe at the great stone．她敬畏地凝视着那块巨石。 近 admiration, reverence, respect, veneration; admire, revere, respect, venerate 反 irreverence, insolence, scorn, superciliousness　蔑视，不敬 派 awesome adj．令人敬畏的
