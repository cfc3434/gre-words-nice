chorale [kəˈrɑːl] 【考法1】 n．赞美诗：a hymn or psalm sung to a traditional or composed melody in church 例 practice a chorale to perform in church　练习吟唱要在教堂表演的赞美诗 近 anthem, canticle, carol, hymn, paean, psalm, spiritual 【考法2】 n．合唱班，合唱团：an organized group of singers 例 a chorale that is regarded as being among the best in the state　一个被认为是国内一流的合唱班 近 choir, chorus, consort, ensemble
