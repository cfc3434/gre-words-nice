preempt [priˈempt] 【考法】 vt．预先占有：to appropriate, seize, or take for oneself before others 例 The naughty children had preempted front-row seats that were reserved for the guests of honor．调皮的孩子们把前排留给贵宾的座位占了。 近 appropriate, arrogate, commandeer, convert, expropriate, seize, usurp 派 preemptive adj．先发制人的；先买的
