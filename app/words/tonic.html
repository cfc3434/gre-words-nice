tonic [ˈtɑːnɪk] 【考法】 adj．滋补的，有益健康的：producing or stimulating physical, mental, or emotional vigor; beneficial to the health of body or mind 例 tonic medicine　补药 近 medicinal, restorative, salubrious, salutary, salutiferous, sanative, wholesome 反 insalubrious, noxious, unhealthful, unhealthy, unwholesome　不健康的
