jettison [ˈdʒetɪsn] 【考法】 vt．放弃，拒绝接受（想法、计划等）：to get rid of as superfluous or encumbering; omit or forgo as part of a plan or as the result of some other decision 例 The Government seems to have jettisoned the bail-out plan．政府似乎已经放弃了这个救市计划。 近 abandon, abdicate, discard, junk, reject, scrap, shed, slough, throw away 反 rescue, salvage　救援，拯救
