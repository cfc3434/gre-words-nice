attune [əˈtuːn] 【考法】 vt．使协调，使和谐：to bring into harmony 例 After years spent in academia, he found it difficult to attune himself to the corporate culture．在学术圈摸爬滚打多年之后，他发现自己很难融入企业文化之中。 近 accommodate, conciliate, conform, coordinate, key, reconcile 反 disharmonize　使不和谐；disarray, disorder, disorganize, disrupt　弄乱，打乱 派 attunement n．调音；协调
