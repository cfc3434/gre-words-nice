noisome [ˈnɔɪsəm] 【考法1】 adj．恶臭的：offensive to the senses and especially to the sense of smell 例 noisome garbage　散发着恶臭的垃圾 近 fetid, foul, malodorous, smelly, stinky 反 ambrosial, aromatic, fragrant, perfumed, redolent, savory, scented, sweet　有香味的 【考法2】 adj．非常令人厌恶的：highly obnoxious or objectionable 例 noisome habits of littering　让人反感的随地乱扔垃圾的坏习惯 近 abhorrent, appalling, disgusting, hideous, loathsome, nauseating, offensive, repugnant, repulsive 反 appealing, captivating, enchanting, fascinating　有吸引力的；agreeable, pleasant　令人愉悦的
