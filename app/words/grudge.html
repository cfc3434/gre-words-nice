grudge [ɡrʌdʒ] 【考法】 n．怨恨，仇恨：a feeling of deep-seated resentment or ill will 例 There has been a grudge between the two families for years．两家之间积怨已深。‖ He has had a deep grudge against her ever since she snubbed him at the dance．自从她在舞会上对他不理不睬之后，他就对她怀有深深的怨念。 近 animosity, animus, antagonism, antipathy, bitterness, gall, hostility, jaundice, malice, rancor, resentment 反 amity　和睦，友好 派 grudging adj．勉强的，不情愿的
