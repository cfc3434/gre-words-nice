iniquity [ɪˈnɪkwəti] 【考法】 n．邪恶，不公正：gross immorality or injustice 例 The use of illegal narcotics is not only a destroyer of personal health but also an iniquity that undermines our society．非法的毒品不仅仅摧残着个人健康，还是一颗危害社会的毒瘤。 近 corruption, depravity, debauchery, evil, infamy, sin, unfairness, wickedness 反 integrity, rectitude　正直；virtue　美德；disinterestedness　公正
