lumber [ˈlʌmbər] 【考法1】 vi．笨拙地行动：to walk or move with heavy clumsiness 例 The elephant lumbered through the jungle．大象缓缓地穿越丛林。 近 flounder, plod, stumble, trudge 反 glide, slide　轻松地滑动 【考法2】 vt．使负担（从而拖累）：to place a weight or burden on 例 lumber the expedition with unnecessary equipment and supplies　不必要的仪器和补给品给此次远征徒增了许多负担 近 burden, encumber, freight, lade, laden, saddle, weight 反 disburden, discharge, disencumber, unlade, unload　卸下，解脱
