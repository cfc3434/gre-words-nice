grandiloquent [ɡrænˈdɪləkwənt] 【考法】 adj．（语言等）浮夸的：a lofty, extravagantly colorful, pompous, or bombastic style, manner, or quality especially in language 例 feel disgusted with his grandiloquent speech　对他浮夸的演讲表示厌恶 近 rhetorical, bombastic, inflated, magniloquent, pretentious 反 secretive　低调的，隐秘的；simple　简单的 派 grandiloquence n．夸张的话语
