reign [reɪn] 【考法】 n．统治权：the right or means to command or control others vi．占统治地位或盛行：to be predominant or prevalent 例 A healthy nation should be governed by the reign of law and not by the will of its chief executive．一个健康的国家应该是法制的，而非由最高领导人的意志统领。‖ They accused him of carrying out a reign of terror．他们指责他搞独裁暴政。‖ Chaos reigned in the classroom．整个教室一片混乱。‖ Panic reigned as the fire spread．随着大火的扩散，恐慌也迅速蔓延。 近 arm, authority, clutch, command, control, dominion, grip, hold, mastery, sway; dominate, domineer, prevail, predominate, rule 反 impotence, impotency, powerlessness　无权，无能
