dread [dred] 【考法】 n．恐惧：great fear especially in the face of impending evil 例 We were filled with dread when we saw the rapids we would be rafting down．当我们看到竹筏驶向激流时，我们的内心被恐惧所占据。 近 alarm, apprehension, consternation, fear, fright, horror, terror, trepidation 反 bravery, courage, dauntlessness, fearlessness, fortitude, intrepidity, stoutness, valor　勇气，无畏 派 dreadful adj．令人恐惧的
