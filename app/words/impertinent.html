impertinent [ɪmˈpзːrtnənt] 【考法1】 adj．无关紧要的：not having a clear decisive relevance to the matter in hand 例 Your résumé needlessly lists extracurricular experiences that are impertinent to the PhD program for which you are applying．你的简历里列出的一些课外活动和你申请的博士项目是毫不相关的。 近 extraneous, immaterial, inapplicable, inapposite, irrelevant 反 germane, pertinent, relevant　相关的；crucial, important, significant　重要的 【考法2】 adj．粗鲁无礼的，大胆的：given to or characterized by insolent rudeness 例 I don't like strangers who ask impertinent questions．我很讨厌那些问无礼问题的陌生人。 近 audacious, bold, brash, brassbound, brassy, brazen, impudent, insolent 反 meek, mousy, retiring, shy, timid　内敛的，胆小的
