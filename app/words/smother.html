smother [ˈsmʌðər] 【考法】 vt．抑制（表达、说出），压制：to refrain from openly showing or uttering 例 He quickly smothered his inappropriate laughter at the funeral ceremony．他很快就控制住了自己在葬礼上不适当的笑声。‖ Management smothered the true facts of the case．管理层隐瞒了案件的真相。 近 bridle, check, choke, control, curb, muffle, quash, quell, repress, squelch, suppress 反 unleash, vent　（感情）爆发，宣泄；express　表达
