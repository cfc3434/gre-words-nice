thick-skinned [ˌθɪkˈskɪnd] 【考法】 adj．冷漠无情的，不顾及他人感受的：largely unaffected by the needs and feelings of other people 例 a cold and thick-skinned person　一个冷漠无情的人 近 affectless, callous, cold-blooded, heartless, indurate, inhumane, insensitive, merciless, obdurate, ruthless 反 charitable, compassionate, humane, kindhearted, merciful, sympathetic, tender, warmhearted　慈善的
