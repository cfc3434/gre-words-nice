raspy [ˈræspi] 【考法】 adj．声音刺耳的：harsh and dry in sound 例 The dying man was speaking in a raspy and barely discernible voice．将死的人在用一种沙哑刺耳且难以听清的语调说话。 近 cacophonous, coarse, croaky, harsh, grating, gravelly, gruff, husky, rasping, rusty, scratchy, throaty 反 mellifluous, sweet　声音甜美的；gentle, soft, tender　声音轻柔的
