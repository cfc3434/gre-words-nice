peeve [piːv] 【考法】 vt．打扰，惹恼：to disturb the peace of mind of (someone) especially by repeated disagreeable acts 例 be peeved by flattery　被拍马屁惹恼 近 aggravate, annoy, bother, exasperate, frost, gall, irk, nettle, pique, rile, ruffle, vex 反 becalm, compose, lull, lullaby, quiet, salve, settle, soothe, still, tranquilize　使镇定 派 peevish adj．易怒的，脾气不好的
