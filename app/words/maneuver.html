maneuver [məˈnuːvər] 【考法】 vt．巧妙地操纵：to guide with adroitness and design or to bring about or secure as a result of skillful management 例 They maneuvered him into signing the contract．他们巧妙地诱使他签下合同。‖ The host maneuvered the conversation so as to avoid the touchy subject of her divorce．主人巧妙地控制着对话的内容，以避免触及有关她离婚的敏感话题。 近 contrive, finagle, finesse, frame, machinate, manipulate, mastermind, negotiate, wangle 反 botch, bungle, fumble, mishandle, muff, scamp, mess up　弄砸
