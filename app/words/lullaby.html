lullaby [ˈlʌləbaɪ] 【考法】 vt．使镇静，使安心：to free from distress or disturbance 例 Reclining peacefully on the deck, he was lullabied by the gentle motion of the ship．他躺卧在甲板上，随着船轻轻的颠簸放松下来。 近 allay, balm, becalm, compose, lull, quiet, salve, settle, soothe, still, tranquilize 反 agitate, discompose, disquiet, disturb, perturb, upset, vex　打扰，扰乱
