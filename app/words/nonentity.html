nonentity [nɑːˈnentəti] 【考法】 n．不重要的人：a person or thing of little consequence or significance 例 She was so quiet she was almost a nonentity at the meeting．她非常沉默，在会议上是个边缘人物。 近 cipher, half-pint, insignificancy, lightweight, nullity, snippersnapper, twerp, whippersnapper, zero, zilch 反 mogul, big shot, big wheel, bigwig, eminence, figure, magnate, personage, somebody　显要人物
