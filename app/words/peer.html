peer [pɪr] 【考法1】 n．同等地位的人，同辈：a person who has equal standing with others 例 stand out among peers　在同辈当中很出众 近 coequal, compeer, coordinate, counterpart, equivalent, fellow, match, parallel 反 inferior　地位更低的人；superior　地位更高的人 派 peerless adj．无与伦比的，不可比拟的 【考法2】 vi．好奇地凝视：to look narrowly or curiously 例 peer at the variety of marine life in the aquarium's huge tank　好奇地注视着巨大水族箱中丰富多样的海洋生物 近 blink, gape, gawk, gaze, goggle, rubberneck, stare 反 glance, glimpse　投去一瞥
