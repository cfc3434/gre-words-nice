minatory [ˈmɪnətɔːri] 【考法】 adj．带来威胁的，有凶兆的：being or showing a sign of evil or calamity to come 例 The novel's protagonist is haunted by a minatory black specter．这部小说的主人公被不祥的黑色幽灵所骚扰。 近 baleful, direful, doomy, foreboding, ill-boding, inauspicious, menacing, portentous, sinister, threatening 反 unthreatening　没有威胁的；reassuring　令人安心的
