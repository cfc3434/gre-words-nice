hedonism [ˈhiːdənɪzəm] 【考法】 n．享乐主义：the doctrine that pleasure or happiness is the sole or chief good in life 例 Their spring break trip to Mexico became an exercise in heedless hedonism．他们春季的墨西哥之行成为了一次未加注意的享乐之旅。 近 carnality, debauchery, sybaritism, voluptuousness 反 abstinence, asceticism, sobriety, temperance　禁欲，节制
