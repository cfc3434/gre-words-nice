designate [ˈdezɪɡneɪt] 【考法1】 vt．任命：to pick (someone) by one's authority for a specific position or duty 例 He has yet to designate his successor as head of the firm．他还没有给公司选定接班人。 近 assign, commission, constitute, nominate, place 反 discharge, dismiss, expel, fire　解雇，开除 【考法2】 vt．命名：to give a name to 例 He was designated Air Jordan by his fans．他被粉丝们称为“飞人乔丹”。 近 denominate, dub, entitle, label, style, term, title
