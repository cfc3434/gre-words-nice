antagonize [ænˈtæɡənaɪz] 【考法1】 vt．与…敌对，反对：to act in opposition to: counteract 例 antagonize a bill　反对一项议案 近 counteract, disagree 反 agree, concede, grant, win over（使）同意 【考法2】 vt．激怒：to incur or provoke the hostility of 例 His remark antagonized his friends．他的评述激怒了他的朋友。 近 aggravate, exasperate, gall, inflame, nettle, provoke, peeve, pique, irritate, rile, roil, chafe, grate, ruffle, vex 反 soothe, defuse, allay, conciliate, propitiate, mitigate, assuage, appease, pacify, placate, calm, settle, subdue, solace, mollify　平息，抚慰
