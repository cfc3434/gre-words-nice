decipher [dɪˈsaɪfər] 【考法1】 vt．破译：to read or interpret (ambiguous, obscure, or illegible matter) 例 We deciphered the hidden message to find out when we were supposed to meet．通过破解暗语，我们得知何时要碰头。 近 break, crack, decrypt, decode 反 cipher, encipher, encode, encrypt　加密 【考法2】 vt．对…有清晰想法，理解，解读：to have a clear idea of 例 a convoluted thriller, the plot of which I was never able to actually decipher　一部错综复杂的恐怖电影，情节我一直没有理解 近 apprehend, perceive, recognize, seize, sense, make out
