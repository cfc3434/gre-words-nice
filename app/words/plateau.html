plateau [plæˈtoʊ] 【考法1】 n．高原：a usually extensive level land area raised sharply above adjacent land on at least one side 例 Qinghai-Tibetan Plateau　青藏高原 近 highland, mesa, tableland, upland 反 lowland　低谷；basin　盆地 【考法2】 n．稳定时期，平台期：a relatively stable level, period, or state 例 reach a plateau of development　达到发展的稳定期 近 equilibrium, stage, stasis
