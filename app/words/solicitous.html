solicitous [səˈlɪsɪtəs] 【考法】 adj．为他人操心的，体谅他人的：given to or made with heedful anticipation of the needs and happiness of others 例 The solicitous husband had already cleaned the house and cooked dinner by the time his wife returned home from work．这个十分体谅妻子的好丈夫在他妻子回家之前就把家里清理干净，还把晚餐都做好了。 近 attentive, considerate, kind 反 heedless, inconsiderate, thoughtless, unthinking　不关心的 派 solicitude n．关切，挂念
