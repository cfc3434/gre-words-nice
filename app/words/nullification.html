nullification [ˌnʌlɪfiˈkeɪʃn] 【考法】 n．（尤指法律条文等正式的）废除，废弃：the doing away with something by formal action 例 the nullification of a treaty　废除条约 近 abolishment, abrogation, annulment, cancellation, invalidation, negation, repeal, rescindment, voiding 反 enactment, legislation　立法；establishment, founding, institution　建立
