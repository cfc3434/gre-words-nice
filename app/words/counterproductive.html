counterproductive [ˌkaʊntərprəˈdʌktɪv] 【考法】 adj．反效果的，阻碍预期目标的：not producing or tending to hinder the attainment of a desired goal 例 Violence as a means to achieve an end is counterproductive.（W. E. Brock）用暴力来达到目标往往是事与愿违的。——W. E．布洛克 近 feckless, hamstrung, ineffective, ineffectual, inefficacious, inefficient, inexpedient 反 effective, effectual, efficacious, efficient, expedient, operant　行之有效的
