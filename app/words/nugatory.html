nugatory [ˈnuːɡətɔːri] 【考法】 adj．无关紧要的：of little or no consequence; inconsequential 例 The book is entertaining, but its contributions to Shakespearean scholarship are nugatory．这本书娱乐性不错，但是对于莎士比亚研究的贡献微不足道。 近 incidental, inconsequential, inconsiderable, insignificant, negligible, trifling, trivial 反 consequential, eventful, important, major, meaningful, momentous, significant, substantial, unfrivolous, weighty　重要的
