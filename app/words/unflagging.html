unflagging [ˌʌnˈflæɡɪŋ] 【考法】 adj．不懈的，不知疲倦的：not declining in strength or vigor 例 I'm greatly indebted to my editor, without whose unflagging efforts this book cannot be published on time．我非常感谢我的编辑，没有他/她的不懈努力这本书不可能如期上市。 近 indefatigable, inexhaustible, tireless, unrelenting, weariless 反 dull, lackadaisical, languid, lethargic, listless, sluggish, torpid　无精打采的 派 unflaggingly adv．不懈地
