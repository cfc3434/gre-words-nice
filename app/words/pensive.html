pensive [ˈpensɪv] 【考法】 adj．沉思的，（尤指）哀思的：given to or marked by long, quiet and often musingly sad thinking 例 Rainy days often put her in a pensive mood．雨天总是让她陷入深深的思考之中。 近 broody, cogitative, meditative, melancholy, musing, reflective, ruminant, ruminative, thoughtful 反 mindless, thoughtless, unreflective　不加思考的
