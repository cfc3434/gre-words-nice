catharsis [kəˈθɑːrsiːz] 【考法】 n．情绪的宣泄、释放：elimination of a complex by bringing it to consciousness and affording it expression 例 The author calls reading a perfect practice of catharsis, one that cleanses him of feelings of fury and frustration．作者声称阅读是一种宣泄情绪的极佳方式：所有的愤怒与失望都随之消失。 近 cleansing, expurgation, lustration, purgation, purification 派 cathartic adj．宣泄情绪的
