compendium [kəmˈpendiəm] 【考法】 n．摘要：a brief summary of a larger work or of a field of knowledge: abstract 例 a compendium of information　资料概要 近 abstract, brief, overview 派 compendious adj．简洁而全面的：concise and comprehensive 例 his compendious knowledge of this subject　他关于这一学科的全面知识 近 concise, brief, laconic, compendiary, succinct
