gauge [ɡeɪdʒ] 【考法1】 n．测量标准：a measurement (as of linear dimension) according to some standard or system 例 polls as a gauge of voter satisfaction　用以衡量选民满意度的投票选举 近 standard, benchmark, criterion, measure, touchstone, yardstick 【考法2】 vt．判定：to determine the capacity or contents of 例 It is hard to gauge his mood．要判断他的情绪很困难。 近 assess, determine, evaluate, figure, measure, scale
