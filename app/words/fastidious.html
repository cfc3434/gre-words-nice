fastidious [fæˈstɪdiəs] 【考法】 adj．挑剔的，极仔细的，追求完美的：possessing or displaying careful, meticulous attention to detail 例 be fastidious about personal appearance and hygiene　极其注意个人外表和卫生 近 demanding, exacting, fussy, hypercritical, squeamish 反 undemanding　不挑剔的；cursory　粗略的，敷衍的；indiscriminate, uncritical　不加区分的
