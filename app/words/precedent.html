precedent [ˈpresɪdənt] 【考法1】 n．先例，前例：an earlier occurrence of something similar 例 a landmark decision that set a legal precedent　在法律上首开先河的重大决议 近 example, instance, model, paradigm, pattern, standard 【考法2】 adj．先前的：prior in time, order, arrangement, or significance 例 Her violent behaviors may be explained by some precedent events in her troubled life．她的暴力行径或许可以由她之前苦难生活中遭遇到的一些事件来解释。 近 antecedent, anterior, former, preceding, previous, prior 反 ensuing　继而发生的 派 unprecedented adj．前所未有的
