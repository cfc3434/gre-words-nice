staunch [stɔːntʃ] 【考法】 adj．忠诚的，坚定的：steadfast in loyalty or principle 例 a staunch believer in the democratic system　民主制度的坚定拥护者 ‖ No matter what happens I will be your staunchest supporters．不论发生什么，我都会是你最忠诚的支持者。 近 constant, dedicated, devoted, devout, faithful, fast, loyal, pious, steadfast, steady, true, true-blue 反 disloyal, inconstant, perfidious, recreant, traitorous, treacherous　不忠诚的；fickle　善变的
