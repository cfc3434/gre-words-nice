hyperbole [haɪˈpзːrbəli] 【考法】 n．夸张：a figure of speech in which exaggeration is used for emphasis or effect 例 The debate was carried on with increasing rhetorical hyperbole．随着辩论的进行，双方修辞当中的夸张越来越多。 近 coloring, embellishment, embroidering, exaggeration, magnification, overstatement 反 understatement　有节制的陈述
