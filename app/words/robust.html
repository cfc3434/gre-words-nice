robust [roʊˈbʌst] 【考法】 adj．精力充沛的；强壮的，健康的：full of health and strength 例 a robust older man who still bicycles 10 miles a day　一个每天骑行10英里的充满活力的老人 近 bouncing, dynamic, energetic, gingerly, hale, potent, strong, sound, vigorous, vital, wholesome 反 delicate, frail, weak　弱不禁风的；ailing, diseased, ill, sick, unfit, unhealthy, unsound　不健康的 派 robustness n．健壮，活力四射
