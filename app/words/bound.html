bound [baʊnd] 【考法1】 n．界限：a real or imaginary point beyond which a person or thing cannot go 例 The language in the novel really is beyond the bounds of decency．这本小说的语言实在太不得体了。 近 environs, limit, confines, perimeter 反 unrestrainedness　无边无际 【考法2】 adj．投入的，坚定的：fully committed to achieving a goal 例 I am bound and determined to write a novel before I turn 30．我下定决心在30岁之前要写本小说。 近 resolute, determined, single-minded, bent on 反 faltering, hesitant, vacillating, wavering, weak-kneed　动摇的 派 boundless adj．无边无际的，无约束的
