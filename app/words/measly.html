measly [ˈmiːzli] 【考法】 adj．少得可怜的，微不足道的：so small or unimportant as to warrant little or no attention 例 The stingy man gave the parking attendant a measly tip．那个吝啬男给了停车保安少得可怜的小费。 近 inconsequential, inconsiderable, insignificant, minute, paltry, peanut, petty, slight, trifling, trivial 反 big, consequential, considerable, significant　大量的，重要的
