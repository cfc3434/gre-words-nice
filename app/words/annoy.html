annoy [əˈnɔɪ] 【考法】 vt．不断烦扰：to disturb or irritate especially by repeated acts 例 Mosquitoes annoy us in the summer．夏天蚊子总是不断烦扰我们。 近 aggravate, bother, chafe, gall, grate, irk, nettle, peeve, pique, rile, ruffle, spite, vex 反 soothe, defuse, allay, conciliate, propitiate, mitigate, assuage, appease, pacify, placate, calm, settle, subdue, solace, mollify　平息，抚慰
