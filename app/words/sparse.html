sparse [spɑːrs] 【考法】 adj．稀疏的，稀少的：less plentiful than what is normal, necessary, or desirable 例 Many slopes are rock fields with sparse vegetation．许多山坡都是不长植物的石坡。 近 exiguous, meager, niggardly, poor, scant, scanty, scarce, skimpy, slender, slim, sparing, stingy 反 abundant, ample, bountiful, copious, generous, liberal, plenteous, plentiful　大量的，丰富的
