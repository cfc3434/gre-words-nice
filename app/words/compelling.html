compelling [kəmˈpelɪŋ] 【考法】 adj．极具说服力的：having the power to persuade 例 make a compelling argument against military intervention　就反对军事干预提出了很有说服力的论证 If you can't present any compelling evidence to prove your innocence, you will be found guilty．如果你找不出任何能证明你清白的有力证据，你就会被定罪。 近 conclusive, convincing, decisive, effective, forceful, persuasive, satisfying, strong, telling 反 feeble, weak　说服力不足的
