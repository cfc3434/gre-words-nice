acute [əˈkjuːt] 【考法1】 adj．敏锐的：marked by keen discernment or intellectual perception especially of subtle distinctions, penetrating 例 an acute thinker　思维敏锐的思想者 近 delicate, fine, keen, perceptive, sensitive 【考法2】 adj．（程度、影响）极强的：extreme in degree, or effect 例 She was experiencing acute distress over the misunderstanding with her best friend．她因与死党之间发生误会而陷入了深深的痛苦中。 近 dreadful, excruciating, profound 反 light, moderate, soft　温和的
