agitate [ˈædʒɪteɪt] 【考法1】 v．煽动，激起：to attempt to arouse public feeling 例 agitate for better conditions　为得到更好的条件而煽动 近 debate, dispute, bat (around or back and forth), hash（over or out）, talk over 【考法2】 vt．使不安：to excite and often trouble the mind or feelings of: disturb 例 There's no need to agitate the patient about little things．没必要因为鸡毛蒜皮的事让患者烦躁。 近 bother, discomfort, discompose, perturb 反 calm, compose, soothe, tranquilize　平息，抚慰
