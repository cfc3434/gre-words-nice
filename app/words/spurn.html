spurn [spзːrn] 【考法】 vt．摈弃，拒绝：to reject with disdain or contempt 例 They spurned his recommendation．他们拒绝了他的推荐。‖ Fiercely independent, the elderly couple spurned all offers of financial help．这对老夫妻极其独立，回绝了所有经济援助。 近 decline, disapprove, negative, refuse, reject, reprobate, repudiate 反 accede, accept, agree, approve, assent, consent, embrace　同意，接受 派 spurned adj．被摒弃的，被拒绝的
