paramount [ˈpærəmaʊnt] 【考法】 adj．最重要的：of chief concern or importance 例 The paramount goal is to restore the colonial-era house with complete historical accuracy．最重要的任务就是十分精确地依照历史重建殖民时期的建筑。 近 cardinal, chief, key, leading, main, predominant, preeminent, primary, principal, supreme 反 ancillary, secondary　次要的；paltry, petty, trifling, trivial　不重要的
