venial [ˈviːniəl] 【考法】 adj．可宽恕的：easily excused or forgiven 例 Taking the restaurant's menu as a souvenir seems like a venial offense．把餐厅的菜单当成纪念品带走似乎是一个可以宽恕的罪行。 近 condonable, excusable, forgivable, pardonable, remissible, remittable 反 heinous, indefensible, inexcusable, mortal, unforgivable, unpardonable　罪大恶极的，不可饶恕的
