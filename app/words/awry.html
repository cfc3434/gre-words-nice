awry [əˈraɪ] 【考法】 adj./adv．出差错的（地）：off the correct or expected course 例 Operation Redwing was initially launched by US special-operation troops in hopes of capturing or killing a Taliban leader but went awry．由美军特种部队发起、代号为“红翼”的军事行动的最初目的是捕获或击杀一名塔利班头目，然而行动在执行过程中出现了差错。 近 amiss, aside, astray, erroneous 反 right, well　无差错的
