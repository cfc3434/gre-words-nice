blast [blæst] 【考法1】 n．爆炸：an explosion or violent detonation vt．炸裂，爆破：to cause to break open or into pieces by or as if by an explosive 例 blast wave of a nuclear bomb　原子弹的冲击波 近 burst, detonation, eruption, outburst; demolish, explode, smash, blow up 反 implosion　向内爆裂 【考法2】 vt．斥责，抨击：to criticize harshly and usually publicly 例 blast the new governor for every little misstep　就每一个小的失误抨击新的执政官 近 abuse, assail, belabor, castigate, lambaste, scathe, vituperate
