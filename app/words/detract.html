detract [dɪˈtrækt] 【考法1】 vt．贬低，降低价值：to diminish the importance, value, or effectiveness of something 例 detract from sth．　贬低某事 近 belittle, depreciate, derogate, disparage, dispraise, write off 反 extol, praise, eulogize, laud, panegyrize　赞美 【考法2】 vt．使分心：to draw the attention or mind to something else 例 Numerous typos in the text detract the reader's attention from the novel's intricate plot．许多打印错误让读者分心，不关注复杂的情节了。 近 divert, call off
