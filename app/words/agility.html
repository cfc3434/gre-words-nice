agility [əˈdʒɪləti] 【考法】 n．（身手）敏捷：quickness, ease and grace in physical activity 例 His agility on the parallel bars has won him several medals．他在双杠上的矫捷身姿助他赢得了数枚奖牌。 近 deftness, dexterity, nimbleness, sleight, spryness 反 awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness, gawkiness, gracelessness, ungainliness（行动）笨拙
