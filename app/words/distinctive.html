distinctive [dɪˈstɪŋktɪv] 【考法1】 adj．完全不同的：being not of the same kind 例 She seems to alternate between two distinctive hairstyles．她的发型似乎在两种截然不同的样式中变换。 近 disparate, dissimilar, distant, distinct, distinguishable, diverse, unalike, unlike 反 identical, indistinguishable, same　相同的；alike, analogous, kindred, like, parallel, similar　相似的 派 distinction n．区别，差别 【考法2】 adj．特征性的，典型的：serving to identify as belonging to an individual or group 例 Jerusalem has a distinctive Middle East flavor．耶路撒冷有着典型的中东风情。 近 characteristic, classic, diagnostic, discriminating, distinguishing, identifying, peculiar, symptomatic, typical 反 atypical, uncharacteristic, untypical　非典型的
