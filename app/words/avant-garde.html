avant-garde [ˌævɑːˈɡɑːrd] 【考法】 n．（尤指艺术上的）先锋派：an intelligentsia that develops new or experimental concepts especially in the arts 例 To this theater world's avant-garde, silent films seemed like a very old-fashioned play whose time had come and gone．对于这个影视先锋派而言，无声电影似乎已是辉煌不再的过时表演方式。 近 forerunner, harbinger, pioneer, precursor, vanguard 反 conservative, diehard　保守者，顽固分子
