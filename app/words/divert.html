divert [daɪˈvзːrt] 【考法1】 vt．使转向：to turn from one course or use to another: deflect 例 divert traffic to a side street　使交通转至辅路 近 deflect, redirect, swing, veer, wheel 反 fix, set, settle　固定 【考法2】 vt．使消遣：to cause (someone) to pass the time agreeably occupied 例 A light comedy divert the tired business executive．一个轻松的喜剧让疲惫的企业主管人员得到了放松。 近 disport, entertain, regale, solace
