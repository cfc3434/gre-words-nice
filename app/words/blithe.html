blithe [blaɪð] 【考法1】 adj．愉快高兴的：of a happy lighthearted character or disposition 例 Everyone loved her for her blithe spirit．所有人都喜欢她开朗的性格。 近 bright, buoyant, gay, jocular, jocund, jovial 反 dour, gloomy, morose, saturnine, sulky, sullen　忧郁的 【考法2】 adj．无忧无虑的；漫不经心的：having or showing freedom from worries or troubles 例 He has a blithe attitude about ever having to earn a living because he knows there's a trust fund in his future．他对生活无忧无虑，因为他知道他的未来有信托基金可以倚靠。 近 debonair, insouciant, lighthearted 反 careworn　焦虑的
