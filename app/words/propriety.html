propriety [prəˈpraɪəti] 【考法】 n．适当，得体：the quality or state of being especially suitable or fitting 例 the propriety of doing sth.　做某事得体 近 appositeness, aptness, felicity, fitness, properness, rightness, seemliness, suitability 反 improperness, impropriety, inappositeness, inappropriateness, inaptness, infelicity, unfitness, unseemliness, unsuitability, wrongness　不得体
