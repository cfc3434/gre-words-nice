reprove [rɪˈpruːv] 【考法】 vt．温和地责备，警告：to scold or correct usually gently or with kindly intent 例 My piano teacher often reproves me for slouching while playing, observing that good posture helps one play better．我的钢琴老师常常会因为懒散的坐姿而批评我，他认为良好的坐姿能让我弹得更好。 近 admonish, blame, chide, criticize, reprimand, reproach 反 lambaste　严厉地责备；commend, eulogize, extol, laud, praise　表扬
