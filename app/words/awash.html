awash [əˈwɔːʃ] 【考法】 adj．（如洪水般）泛滥的：filled, covered, or completely overrun as if by a flood 例 The program is currently awash in submissions and will not be accepting any more until next term．该项目的申请人数过多，因此下一轮之前不会再接受任何申请。 近 abounding, abundant, flush, fraught, replete, swarming, teeming, thronging 反 dearth, insufficient, scant　匮乏的
