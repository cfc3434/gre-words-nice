revere [rɪˈvɪr] 【考法】 vt．（尤指对神的）尊崇，尊敬：to offer honor or respect to（someone）as a divine power 例 He is revered for his valor．他因为英勇而受到尊敬。‖ In some cultures people revere their ancestors, even leaving food offerings for them．在一些文化中，人们非常敬重先人，甚至将一些食物留下来供他们享用。 近 adore, deify, glorify, esteem, regard, respect, venerate, worship 反 blaspheme, desecrate, profane, violate　亵渎 派 reverence n．尊重；reverent adj．恭敬的，虔诚的
