concrete [ˈkɑːŋkriːt] 【考法】 adj．事实性的，明确的：existing in fact and not merely as a possibility 例 Concrete evidence, and not just a theory, must be presented at a trial．在法庭上需要出示确凿的证据，而非臆测。 近 effective, existent, factual, genuine, real, true, very, de facto 反 conjectural, hypothetical, ideal, platonic, suppositional, theoretical　猜测的，理论上的
