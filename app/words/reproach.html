reproach [rɪˈproʊtʃ] 【考法】 vt．批评，责备：to express disapproval, criticism, or disappointment in (someone) 例 reproached by their mother for untidiness　因为邋遢而被他们的母亲批评 近 admonish, berate, castigate, chide, condemn, denounce, lambaste, rebuke, reprimand, reprove, upbraid 反 commend, eulogize, extol, laud, praise　表扬 派 irreproachable adj．无可指责的
