exterminate [ɪkˈstзːrmɪneɪt] 【考法】 vt．根除，消灭：to get rid of completely usually by killing off 例 We hope that the fumigant exterminates the whole colony of cockroaches, for any survivors may be resistant to any poison．我们希望所有的蟑螂都能被这种杀虫熏剂根除，因为任何幸存者都可能产生抗药性。 近 annihilate, decimate, efface, eradicate, expunge, extirpate, liquidate, obliterate, raze, sweep, wipe out 反 conserve, preserve, protect　保护；rescue, save　拯救 派 extermination n．消灭
