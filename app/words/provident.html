provident [ˈprɑːvɪdənt] 【考法1】 adj．节俭的：frugal; economical 例 It is possible to be provident without being miserly．人可以做到节俭但不吝啬。 近 economical, sparing, thrifty, farsighted, provident, scrimpy 反 profligate　挥霍的 【考法2】 adj．有远见的：having or showing awareness of and preparation for the future 例 Her provident measures kept us safe while we waited out the hurricane．她之前有远见的准备措施让我们在等待飓风结束的期间安然无恙。 近 farseeing, farsighted, forehanded, foreseeing, forethoughtful, prescient, proactive, visionary 反 half-baked, half-cocked, improvident, myopic, shortsighted　目光短浅的
