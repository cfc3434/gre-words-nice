humiliate [hjuːˈmɪlieɪt] 【考法】 vt．羞辱，使丧失尊严：to reduce to a lower position in one's own eyes or others' eyes 例 He humiliated me in front of my parents and I swear to retaliate．他在我的父母面前让我难堪，我发誓要报复他。 近 abase, chasten, cheapen, debase, degrade, demean, discredit, disgrace, dishonor, shame, sink, smirch 反 aggrandize, canonize, deify, elevate, exalt　使崇高 派 humiliation n．羞辱，蒙羞
