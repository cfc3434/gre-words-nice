abreast [əˈbrest] 【考法】 adj．熟知的：up to a particular standard or level especially of knowledge of recent developments 例 She tried to keep abreast of the latest fashion trends．她总是尽可能地跟上时尚的潮流。 近 acquainted, conversant, informed, knowledgeable, versed, well-informed 反 ignorant, unacquainted, unfamiliar, uninformed, unknowledgeable　无知的，不熟悉的
