pastiche [pæˈstiːʃ] 【考法1】 n．（带嘲讽的）模仿：a literary, artistic, musical, or architectural work that imitates the style of previous work, often with satirical intent 例 a pastiche of expensive interior design　高档室内设计的模仿 近 burlesque, caricature, imitation, parody, spoof, travesty 反 original work　原作 【考法2】 n．大杂烩：a pasticcio of incongruous parts; a hodgepodge 例 a pastiche of dishes from many countries　来自众多国家的饮食杂烩 近 agglomerate, collage, hodgepodge, jumble, jungle, medley, montage, motley, salad, variety, welter
