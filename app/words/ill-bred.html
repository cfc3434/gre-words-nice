ill-bred [ˈɪlˈbred] 【考法】 adj．没有教养的，没有礼貌的：badly brought up or showing bad upbringing: impolite 例 Only an ill-bred, conceited person would demand that everyone cater to his whims．只有一个没有教养、狂妄自大的人才会要求所有人都去迎合他的想法。 近 discourteous, disrespectful, impertinent, impolite, inconsiderate, rude, uncivil, ungracious, unmannerly 反 civil, considerate, courteous, genteel, gracious, mannerly, polite, urbane, well-bred　有礼貌的，得体的
