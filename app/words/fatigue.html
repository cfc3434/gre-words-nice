fatigue [fəˈtiːɡ] 【考法】 n．疲惫：weariness or exhaustion from labor, exertion, or stress 例 The day-long battle against the blaze left firefighters in a state of utter fatigue．与大火一整天的斗争使得消防员们处于极度疲惫的状态。 近 collapse, exhaustion, lassitude, tiredness, weariness 反 refreshment, rejuvenation, revitalization　恢复活力
