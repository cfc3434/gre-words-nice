conviction [kənˈvɪkʃn] 【考法1】 n．深信，确信：the state of being convinced 例 a warrior of strong conviction　一名信念坚定的战士 近 assurance, certainty, certitude 反 dubiety, incredulity　怀疑；uncertainty, incertitude　不确定性 【考法2】 n．证明有罪：state of being found or proved guilty 例 evidence that led to the suspect's conviction　证明嫌疑犯有罪的证据 近 sentence 反 acquittal　宣告无罪 派 convict v．宣判有罪
