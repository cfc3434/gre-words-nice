magnitude [ˈmæɡnɪtuːd] 【考法】 n．重要，重大：greatness in significance or influence 例 The magnitude of the issue is severely underestimated．这个事件的重要性被严重低估了。 近 account, consequence, importance, moment, momentousness, significance, weight, weightiness 反 insignificance, littleness, puniness, slightness, smallness, triviality　不重要
