torrid [ˈtɔːrɪd] 【考法1】 adj．酷热的：intensely hot 例 the dry, torrid summers in southern Arizona　南亚利桑那州干燥、炎热的夏天 近 hot, scalding, scorching, sweltering 反 arctic, bone-chilling, freezing, frigid, frozen, glacial, icy　寒冷的 【考法2】 adj．热情的，情感深厚的：having or expressing great depth of feeling 例 torrid love affair　炙热的爱 近 ardent, burning, fervid, flaming, glowing, impassioned, incandescent, intense, passionate, vehement 反 dispassionate, emotionless, impassive, unemotional　冷漠的
